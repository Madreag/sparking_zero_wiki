## 1. Episode Battle (Story) Mode

### 1.1 Campaigns — the 8 launch protagonists

→ Namek → Android/Cell → Majin Buu → Beerus/Frieza Resurrection → Future Trunks → Universe Survival).

General mechanic: routes unlock via (a) **dialogue choices** that alter the story, or (b) **harder
secondary win conditions** (usually "defeat X within the time limit" / "win taking minimal damage" /
"win without transforming/Spirit Bomb"). Alternate win conditions are deliberately harder than the
stage's default clear condition.

### 1.4 Episode Battle rewards

- **15,000 Zeni** per *new* Episode Battle mission on first clear (TheGamer farm guide).
- First-clear unique rewards (outfits, character unlocks, illustrations). Duplicate/already-owned
  rewards auto-convert to Zeni.
- Best farm: replay **"Super Saiyan Goku Awakens!"** (Namek arc) — its first-clear reward
  **Goku (Z-Mid) Outfit 3** converts to **300,000 Zeni** on every subsequent replay (Game8).
- No community-published *total* Zeni sum for completing all 8 campaigns (see Gaps).

## 2. Custom Battle Mode (system facts)

Exclusive to Sparking! ZERO. Reached via Main Menu → (since Jan 2026 patch) **"Ultimate Battle"** section.

**Three creation modes:**
1. **Simple Edit** — guided/simplified, has its own tutorial.
2. **Normal Edit** — full editor; default starting scenario is Goku vs Frieza on Namek.
3. **Copy Edit** — duplicate an existing battle (your own creation OR a dev **Bonus Battle**) and tweak everything.

**Editable parameters (Normal Edit):**
- Battle Character roster + **guest characters** (for cutscene-only appearances).
- Per-participant details: stats, transformations, equipped items, CPU level.
  **Max CPU level raised to 21** (Jan 2026 patch; was lower at launch).
- **Trigger → Effect logic system:** e.g. `IF Time Elapsed ≥ 60s THEN force active character to switch`.
  One trigger can chain **multiple effects** (force switch + stat boost + force into Sparking! Mode).
- **Special Match Settings / Conditional Win** effects: "Defeat a specific character", "Time Elapsed", etc.
- **3D scene editor:** poses, facial expressions, camera, intro/outro cinematic cuts (within the chosen scenario's bounds).
- After clearing a **Bonus Battle**, its used effects unlock for copying into your own creations.

**Sharing:** upload/download via **World Library** (online; requires latest patch).

### 2.1 Official Bandai-made battles = "Bonus Battles" (with Zeni rewards)

Bonus Battles are the developer-authored custom battles (preset characters, dialogue, win conditions).
They **double as character-unlock content** and pay tiered Zeni by difficulty class. **First-clear** rewards
(Player EXP always included):

| Difficulty class | Bonus Battle count (Game8 table) | Zeni per first clear |
|------------------|----------------------------------|----------------------|
| Low-Class Warrior | 10 | **20,000** |
| High-Class Warrior | 14 | **25,000** |
| Elite Saiyan Warrior | 10 | **30,000** |
| Legendary Super Saiyan | 3 | **35,000** |

Total listed Bonus Battles ≈ **37** (plus "Battles Under Certain Conditions" variants). Some Bonus Battles
chain-unlock others (e.g. *Hand over Kakarot!* unlocks *Who are you Callin' Number One?!*).

## 3. Unlock Economy (Zeni / progression)

**Currency:** Zeni. **No documented hard wallet cap** in community guides (likely internal cap, number not published).

### 3.1 Character shop

- Base roster ≈ **182 playable entries**; of these **47 "base" characters are shop-purchasable**.
- **Buying one base character unlocks ALL its variants/transformations/fusions** (you never pay per form).
- The 47 can alternatively be unlocked free via Episode Battles, Bonus Battles, offline tournaments,
  or Dragon Ball wishes (Shenron / Porunga / Super Shenron).
- **Per-character price range ≈ 30,000 – 120,000 Zeni.**

| Example character | Shop price (Zeni) |
|-------------------|-------------------|
| Videl | 30,000 |
| Goku (Teen) | 45,000 |
| Kale | 60,000 |
| Goku (GT) / Goku Black | 75,000 |
| Frost / Dyspo | 90,000 |
| Majin Vegeta | 105,000 |
| Goku (Super) Ultra Instinct -Sign- | 120,000 |
| Gogeta (GT) SS4 | 120,000 |

- **Total to buy the entire shop roster ≈ 3,000,000 Zeni** (PowerPyx; ScreenRant corroborates "almost 3 million").
  In practice far less is spent because many unlock free via PVE.
- **Goku (Mini)** is the ONE base-roster character not buyable in-game — it's the **pre-order bonus** (DLC).

### 3.3 Dragon Ball wishes (alt unlock path)

Collecting Dragon Balls in battle summons Shenron / Porunga / Super Shenron to wish for characters,
costumes, or Zeni — an alternative to the shop. (Associated achievements at §5.)
