# Sparking! ZERO — Current Meta, PvP, Tier Lists & Ranked (June 2026)

> **Currency note.** Every tier claim below is labeled with the patch/source it comes from. The game is on the **May 26, 2026 Free Update** as of this writing (latest build cited ~June 5–8, 2026). Game8's tier list — usually a go-to — is **frozen at November 2024** and is treated here as history only. The most current *quantitative* source is **sparkingzerometa.com**, whose own banner says "Site reflects current patch." Numbers from it are model scores (points), not raw in-game damage, so they're used as **relative ranking signals**, cross-referenced against the June 2026 propelrc list and community lists.

## 1. TL;DR — Current S-Tier (post-May 26 2026 patch)

**Singles (standardized HP, no DP damage scaling — see §4):** the fusions and a handful of divine/berserker picks rule.
- **Vegito (SSGSS)**, **Gogeta (GT) SS4**, **Broly (Z) Legendary SS**, **Broly (Super) SS Full Power**, **Jiren Full Power**, **Whis**, **Ultra Instinct Goku**, **Gogeta (Super) SSGSS**, **Kale (Berserk)**, **Gohan Beast**, **Toppo (God of Destruction)**, **Super Vegito**.

**DP Battle (DP-efficiency meta — value per point):** cheap bruisers and healers that "overperform their DP" dominate.
- **Gohan Beast**, **Master Roshi (Max Power)**, **Yajirobe**, **Recoome**, **Broly (Z) LSSJ**, **Broly (Super) SSFP**, **Spopovich**, **Kale (Berserk)**, **Mini Vegeta (SS3/SS2)** — note these are different from the Singles list because DP rewards points-per-DP, not raw strength.

**Biggest meta shift of 2025→2026:** the **May 26 2026 patch** is the largest balance overhaul since launch. It (a) **gutted spam/auto-defense play** — Afterimage Strike now ends on Super Counter, **Auto-reflect restricted to DP7+ only**, Perception reworked to cost 2 skill stock, Rush Ki Blast/Blast combo damage cut; (b) **split Singles and DP into genuinely different games** — Singles now has **standardized HP and no DP damage scaling**, while DP **widened** the strength gap between high- and low-DP characters; (c) removed **heal-on-transform** in Singles (now a permanent attack buff instead). Combined with the **Giant nerfs** and the late-2025 **Yajirobe senzu nerf**, the cheese-heavy 2025 meta is significantly tamed.

## 4. Ranked System — Numbers (official)

**Mode split (as of May 26 2026 patch — official Bandai notice):**
- **Single Battle:** **DP damage scaling REMOVED**; **all characters have standardized/equal HP**. Goal: let players use a wider variety of characters. → This is why low-DP cheese (Yajirobe/Roshi) is bad in Singles now but the *Singles* tier list is dominated by raw-kit characters (fusions).
- **DP Battle:** scaling **revised so DP differences more clearly reflect strength** — i.e., the gap between a DP7 and DP3 character is now *wider*. Team-battle rules unchanged. **Auto-reflect locked to DP7+** during Sparking! Mode.
- **DP totals** (offline/player match/World Tournament/training) selectable: **10 / 15 / 20** (settings now persist). **Ranked DP queue standard remains 15 DP** for a 3-character team (per propelrc/community).

**Ranked ladder & seasons (official, 25 May 2026 notice):**
- Tiers (low→high): **D → C → B → A → S → Z**. Each appears to have sub-divisions (e.g. **B1/B3/B5**, **C5**, **D5**) used in the reset mapping.
- **Season 0** (the launch-era open period) **ends June 30 2026, 08:00 UTC**; final rank → cosmetic **title** reward.
- **Season 1** starts **July 1 2026, 08:00 UTC**, runs **~3 months**.
- **Seasonal soft reset** (your new starting rank based on last season's rank):
  | End-of-season rank | Resets to |

## 5. DP Battle Team Comps & DP Math (15-DP standard)

DP budget = **15** for 3 characters (standard ranked DP queue). Core principle post-May 26: **1 premium carry + cheap value/utility**, because DP-efficiency now matters more and auto-reflect needs DP7+.

**DP costs of key picks** (community/propelrc; DP shown as the in-game point cost 1–10):
- 10-DP "gods": SS4 Gogeta (GT), Vegito SSGSS, Gogeta Super SSGSS, UI Goku, Jiren FP.
- ~7–9 DP: Gohan Beast (9), Orange Piccolo (8), Cell Max (9), Beerus, Whis (low DP but god-tier mobility — Whis is famously cheap), Goku Black Rosé.
- Cheap value (1–4 DP): Yajirobe, Recoome, Saibaman (1), Master Roshi, Spopovich, Mini Vegeta forms, Frieza Force Soldier.

**Representative competitive structures (2026, post-patch):**
1. **"Premium + value" 15-DP** — one 10-DP fusion carry (e.g. SS4 Gogeta GT or Vegito SSGSS) + ~5 DP of cheap DP7+ bodies for auto-reflect access and chip. Example math: 10 (SS4 Gogeta) + 5 split across two cheap DP7-eligible bruisers. Most common ladder shell.
2. **GT Ultra Pressure Duo** (PixelNitro 2026) — GT/Ultra-Instinct-centric, constant Z-Burst pressure, high damage, one primary finisher.
3. **Divine Pressure / Control** (PixelNitro 2026) — Beerus/Whis/Black resource-domination + sustain; slow early but dominant late.
4. **Super-Armor Counter Shell** — armor/punish anchors; predictable but strong defense.
5. **3×5-DP "value spam"** — three ~5-DP overperformers (e.g. Gohan SH SS, Kale, Mini Vegeta SS2). Strong on the efficiency model, but each body is DP<7 so **loses auto-reflect** under the new rule — weaker than it was pre-May-26.

**Cheese / ban-worthy strats (current status):**
- **Yajirobe senzu stall** — *largely dead* in Singles (standardized HP, and senzu cost 6 / start 0 stock since first patch; Ki-recovery nerfed May 26). Still a niche DP tank.
- **Giant wall/AoE lock** (Anilaza/Orange Piccolo Giant/Dr. Wheelo) — *nerfed* May 26 (less melee damage, more whiff recovery, take more melee damage, slower back-dash; standard charge Blasts now damage+knockback giants). Still threatening vs unprepared opponents.
- **Auto-reflect turtling on cheap characters** — *killed* for DP<7 (May 26).
- There is **no official ban list**; tournaments historically used **preset/curated character pools** (see §7) rather than bans.
