| Metric | Value | Note |
|---|---|---|

## 2. DLC pack timeline

> IMPORTANT date nuance: dates below are **PC / PS5 / Xbox**. Nintendo Switch & Switch 2 ports launched later and the *entire* Season Pass arrived on Switch around **May 27 / June 27 2026** (Hero of Justice DLC1 on Switch: Feb 20 2026). The Switch dates are NOT the original release dates.

| Pack | PC/PS5/Xbox date | Price (standalone) | # chars (slots) | Source / Era |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Season Pass 1** (bundles DLC 1-3 + Shallot extra + Shenron bonus) | sold from launch | **$34.99** | 26 total | — |
| DLC 1 — **HERO OF JUSTICE Pack** | Jan 23 2025 (SP early access Jan 20) | **$17.99** | 11 (#183-193) | Movie: *Super: Super Hero* (2022) |
| DLC 2 — **Dragon Ball DAIMA: Character Pack 1** | Apr 24 2025 | ~$9.99 (est.) | 8 (#194-201) | DAIMA |
| Extra — **Shallot (DRAGON BALL LEGENDS)** | Jun 26 2025 | free w/ SP / standalone | 1 (#202) | *Dragon Ball Legends* mobile |
| DLC 3 — **Dragon Ball DAIMA: Character Pack 2** | Sep 24 2025 (early access Sep 21) | ~$9.99 (est.) | 6 (#203-208) | DAIMA (+ First Demon World stage) |
| **Super Limit-Breaking NEO** (= "Season Pass 2" / "DLC 4") | **UNRELEASED** — expected **Summer 2026** | not announced | "over 30" (27 revealed, #209-235) | Mixed: DB / Z / GT / movies (+ new mode "Limit Breaker Journey", stages, costumes) |

Notes:
- Hero of Justice drew fan criticism for price-to-content: 11 slots but only 4 distinct characters (Gohan SH, Piccolo SH, Gamma 1, Gamma 2, Cell Max) — the rest are forms.
- Season Pass officially advertised "4 DLC packs, more than 20 playable characters." The 4th item is the Shallot extra; DLC 1-3 + Shallot = 26 slots delivered.
- Super Limit-Breaking NEO was teased Oct 2 2025, partially shown Jan 24 2026 (10 chars), and formally named + expanded Apr 19 2026 (18 more shown). Marketing line: **"over 30 new playable characters."**

## 5. DP changes from patches (old → new)

**No officially documented per-character numeric DP changes were found.** Balance patches (notably the April 2025 update and the May 26 2026 free update) changed *how DP works*, not individual character DP values:

| Patch | Change | Type |
|---|---|---|
| Apr 2025 update | DP Battle total cap selectable (10 / 15 / 20); setting retained after match | Rule |
| May 26 2026 free update | "Differences in DP between characters made more pronounced in all modes (online + offline)" — widens the practical gap between e.g. DP7 and DP3 | Weighting |
| May 2026 patch (community-summarized) | Auto-reflect during Sparking! Mode restricted to **DP7+** characters (nerf to low-DP picks); UI Goku Smash Ki Blast cooldown up; Gogeta (Super) SSGSS Smash Ki Blast homing/damage adjusted | Mechanic tied to DP tier |

So DP **tiers gained/lost mechanical privileges**, but a given fighter's printed DP number (per the current Fandom DP lists) matches its launch value in the cases checked. Treat "no DP renumbering" as the working conclusion; a full historical diff of every slot's DP across every patch was not published by any source found.

## 6. Stat differentiators / class tags (qualitative — game does not publish HP numbers)

Sparking! ZERO does **not** expose numeric health/stat values in-game or in official material; the cost (DP) system is the only published balancing number. Known class/trait tags that affect play:

- **Giant-class** (oversized hitbox, super armor on many moves, can't be grabbed normally, takes a board slot weight): Great Ape Vegeta, Great Ape Baby, Hirudegarn, Lord Slug Giant Form, Orange Piccolo Giant Form, Giant Gomah, Cell Max, Fusion Android 13, Anilaza, (upcoming Super 17).
- **Android** (ki does not deplete from natural drain the same way / "infinite ki" behavior on some, e.g. Androids 16/17/18, Cell line, Gammas, Hell Fighter 17): listed in roster with "Android" note above.
- **Fusion** (time-limited canon but unlimited in-game; high DP): see Section 4.
- **Super Armor** moves are character-specific (heavies like Broly, Recoome, Nappa, Bojack) rather than a global tag.
- HP/defense differences exist (heavier characters tank more) but are **not numerically published** — any HP figures circulating are community estimates, not official.
