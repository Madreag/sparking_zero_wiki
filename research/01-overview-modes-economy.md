### "Random Dragon Ball" tournament rule
Within **World Tournament** custom rules, players can be **assigned a random fighter** instead of choosing — combined with the iconic tournament presets, this is the community-referred "random" ruleset. The Dragon Ball *collection* mechanic itself (Shenron wishes) is **not** tied to ranked PvP; Dragon Balls are earned via offline battles, Episode completion, and challenge lists (see §3).

### DP (Destruction Points) examples — Ranked/DP Battle
- Total budget: **15 DP**; team max **5 characters**; each character usable once.
- Example costs: Goku (Z-Mid) = **5 DP**; Ultra Instinct Goku = **9 DP**. Higher DP = stronger character.

### Dragon Ball collection — Shenron Wishes system

Three dragons, each needing its own set of 7 Dragon Balls. **Shenron** = easiest (1 wish). **Porunga** = mid-rarity but grants **3 wishes** per summon. **Super Shenron** = rarest, most options (tied to Episode/saga completion). Ultimate Edition includes one free Shenron + one free Super Shenron wish.

| Wish | Shenron | Porunga | Super Shenron | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **I want more Zeni** | ✔ **300,000** | ✔ **120,000** (×3 wishes) | ✔ **1,000,000** | Instant Zeni |
| **I want you to raise my Player Level** | ✔ | ✔ | ✔ | Random level-ups (none if L20) |
| **I want more titles** | ✔ | ✔ | ✔ | Choose a Player-Card title (Shenron pool incl. King / Delightful Lover / King of the World; Porunga: World Domination / Moneybags / Fancy Necklace; up to **11** title options total) |
| **I want new outfits** | ✔ | ✖ | ✔ | Choose 1 of **4** outfits: Desert Bandit Yamcha, Jackie Chun (Master Roshi), Goku (Super) outfit 4, Vegeta (Super) outfit 4 |
| **I want ability items** | ✖ | ✔ | ✔ | Choose 1 of **6**: Super Transformation, Broly's Ring, Mirage, Water's Blessing, Guard Master, Miracle Master |
| **I want more characters** | ✖ | ✖ | ✔ | Unlock **Goku Black** or **Fused Zamasu** (also buyable at L20) |
| **I want to complete an Episode Battle** | ✔ (only after failing one) | ✖ | ✖ | Grants 1 **Dragon Orb** (eases a secondary objective) |

> Cross-checked across GameRant, Gameland.gg, Deltias, PCGamesN, Destructoid, ProGameGuides, PowerPyx — all agree on the **300k / 120k / 1,000,000** Zeni split.

## 4. Online Infrastructure

| Topic | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Cross-play (full)** | **NOT supported** across PS5 ↔ Xbox ↔ PC ↔ Switch. Confirmed at launch and still absent in 2026. Devs called it "difficult rather than impossible." |
| **Cross-play (partial)** | Switch 2 update (Feb 2026) enabled **Switch ↔ Switch 2** cross-play + enhanced visuals on the Switch-family only. |
| **Netcode** | Online uses delay/peer-based networking (community reports; the May 26 2026 patch added latency/connection-stability tuning). Bandai has **not** published an official "rollback" claim — treat as **unverified**. |
| **Ranked structure** | **26 ranks**, D5 (lowest) → Rank Z (top). Two queues: Single Battle, DP Battle (15 DP / 5 chars). Matchmaking by cost restriction; leaderboards. |
| **Ranked seasons** | As of June 2026 still labeled **"Season 0"** Ranked. Current Season 0 period **ends June 30, 2026 @ 08:00 UTC**; players receive **titles** based on final rank as Season 0 rewards. (No formal Season 1 ladder confirmed yet.) |
| **Player counts (Steam, June 2026)** | **~1,003 concurrent live**; all-time peak **122,554 (Oct 11, 2024)**. Steam is a minority of the playerbase (game is multi-platform). |
