A **Shenron Wish** is granted by collecting **7 Dragon Balls in battle** and summoning one of three dragons. The **"I want more Zeni"** wish pays **300,000 from Shenron** (easiest, 1 wish), **120,000 per wish from Porunga** (mid-rarity, **3 wishes** per summon), and **1,000,000 from Super Shenron** (rarest). Other wishes grant **titles** (up to 11 options), **outfits** (4), **ability items** (6, Porunga/Super Shenron only), or unlock **Goku Black / Fused Zamasu** (Super Shenron only). There's also a Shenron-only **"complete an Episode Battle"** wish (after failing one) that grants a Dragon Orb. Optimal strategy in [[zeni-economy-guide]].
