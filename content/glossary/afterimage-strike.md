**Afterimage Strike** (AIS) is the Blast Skill that **auto-dodges every melee attack** for a duration, costing **3 skill stocks**. At launch it ran **15 seconds** and was the #1 degenerate stall; the **Dec 11, 2024** patch cut it to **10 seconds** and made it end on a [[perception|Perception]], the **Jun 23, 2025** patch made it also **end if the user is hit by a** [[super-counter|Super Counter]], and the **May 26, 2026** patch confirmed that wording (plus a small buff: it can now evade unblockable charge Blasts). It's **nerfed but still alive** — strong against players who can't Super Counter. See [[cheese-tech-status-board]].
