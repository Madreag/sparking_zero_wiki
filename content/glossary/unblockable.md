**Unblockable** attacks are flagged (the datamine's **"Impossible Guard"** property) so that **guarding does not stop them** — the defender must **dodge or counter** instead. These were a guaranteed-damage staple of older mix-ups, but patches dismantled the free version: **Jun 23 2025** made unblockable Smash Ki Blasts **Vanish-evadable**, **Sep 22 2025** made unblockable Rush Blasts **evadable with auto-evasion skills**, and **May 26 2026** let auto-evasion skills evade **unblockable charge Blasts**. The notable holdout is **Cell Max's Max Bomb** (made unblockable Jan 2026), though Cell Max itself is [[giant-class|giant-nerfed]]. See [[cheese-tech-status-board]].
