**Super Counter** is the universal answer to front rush pressure: input **↑ + Rush Attack** on the exact frame a hit connects to instantly reverse positions at **zero cost**. The success window is community-measured at **~2 frames (~33 ms)** (a minority argue up to 4f); no official value exists. Because it is the only **free** escape, it defines high-rank play. Two nerfs tamed mash-fishing: a **longer lockout after a failed attempt** (Jan 20 2025) and **no ki recovery during the attempt** (May 26 2026). It also ends an active [[afterimage-strike|Afterimage Strike]]. See [[super-counter]] (mechanic page).
