**Ki Blast** is the basic ranged energy shot, costing roughly **16-22% of one ki bar per shot** (20% typical for Normal class; **16% for Ki-Blast class**; some 22%). The number of queued shots is character-specific (3-8 for most; ki-blast androids/Gammas effectively 999). The **May 26, 2026** patch nerfed **Rush Ki Blast spam** — **reduced damage + stronger consecutive-hit scaling** — while *increasing* rapid-fire ki-blast damage and letting standard charge Blasts damage [[giant-class|giants]]. See [[ki-sickness]] for what happens when you over-spend, and [[unblockable]] for unblockable variants.
