**Senzu Bean** is **Yajirobe's** signature skill: it **fully restores his health**. At launch it cost **5 skill stocks** with a **2-stock start**, enabling an infinite heal-stall that made Yajirobe a meta menace. The **first balance patch (Oct 2024)** raised the cost to **6 stocks**, dropped his **starting stock to 0**, and shaved ~½ a health bar — killing the loop. The **May 26, 2026** patch further **reduced his Ki recovery**. With standardized [[singles|Singles]] HP also removing his tankiness, Senzu-stall is **dead as a meta**; Yajirobe is a niche [[dp|DP]] body now. See [[cheese-tech-status-board]].
