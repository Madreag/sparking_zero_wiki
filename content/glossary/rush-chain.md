**Rush Chain** is the basic melee string: **□ (x1-4 max) + △** into a heavy finisher. A **longer chain does more damage** but creates **more escape windows** for the opponent and grants the **victim more skill-stock recovery when hit from behind** — so max-length chains aren't always optimal. The **May 26, 2026** patch made it a key anti-cheese tool: a **Rush Chain can be executed even while attacks are being auto-dodged** by [[afterimage-strike|UI Goku / Whis]] — the answer to launch-era auto-dodge. See [[smash-attack]] and [[cheese-tech-status-board]].
