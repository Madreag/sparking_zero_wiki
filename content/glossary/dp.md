**DP (Destruction Points)** is the cost system that balances [[singles|DP Battle]] team-building. Each character has a fixed DP cost on a **1-10 scale**, and you assemble a team of up to 3-5 characters under a budget — **15 DP** is the standard ranked cap (10/15/20 selectable offline). **Mr. Satan** is the only **DP1** character; the five **DP10** picks are **Beerus, Whis, Vegito SSGSS, Gogeta Super SSGSS, and Gogeta GT SS4**. Since the **May 26, 2026** patch, **auto-reflect is locked to DP7+** characters and the practical strength gap between DP tiers was **widened**. See [[dp-gap]] and [[dp-team-value-math]].
