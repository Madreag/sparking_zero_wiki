**DP Gap** is the difference in [[dp|DP]] cost between two characters, and it carries real mechanical weight. The **May 26, 2026** patch "made differences in DP between characters **more pronounced in all modes**" — so a high-DP pick now hits **relatively harder** against a cheap one. In **DP Battle only**, the **"Big Impact"** item turns the gap into damage: **+500 at 1 DP difference, +250 per additional DP** when your attacker out-DPs the defender. The gap also gates defense: **auto-reflect is restricted to DP7+** in [[sparking-mode|Sparking! Mode]]. This is the core of [[dp-team-value-math|DP team value math]].
