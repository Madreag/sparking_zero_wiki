**Instant Sparking** is a Blast Skill that puts you straight into [[sparking-mode|Sparking! Mode]], **skipping the gauge charge**, for **4 skill stocks** (raised from **3 at launch** in the Jan 20 2025 patch). It enables surprise ultimate/boosted-damage combos from neutral. The **May 26, 2026** patch made it riskier: like any Sparking activation, **it now lowers your defense for a window** afterward, so you're exposed on the pop. See [[instant-sparking]] (mechanic page) and [[skill-count]].
