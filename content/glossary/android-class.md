**Android-Class** characters have **"infinite ki"** behavior but split into two types. **Infinite-ki Androids (16, 17, 18, Super 17)** have **ki charge speed 0** — they **cannot manually charge ki** — but have effectively unlimited ki blasts and **start +1 skill stock**. **Ki-drain Androids (19, Dr. Gero)** also can't charge, **start +1 skill stock** (datamined `initialSkillStock` = **1**, not +2), and have **huge throw damage** (3,594 / 3,875) reflecting their absorb-on-grab identity. The Dec 11 2024 patch killed their infinite ascend/descend stall by making those movements cost ki; later patches let their Super Perception vs a beam trigger [[sparking-mode|Sparking! Mode]].
