**Whiff Punish** means hitting an opponent during the **recovery frames of an attack they missed**. The **May 26, 2026** patch made whiff-punishing more rewarding by **widening whiff/recovery windows**: [[giant-class|giants]] now have **increased whiff recovery**, and **failed** defensive options are punishable too — a missed [[super-counter|Super Counter]] incurs a **longer lockout**, and a missed [[perception|Perception]] leaves recovery. The skill is to bait an attack, let it whiff, and convert during recovery with a [[rush-chain|Rush Chain]] or [[smash-attack|Smash]].
