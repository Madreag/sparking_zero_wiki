**Revenge Counter** (R3 / right-stick press) is the skill-stock panic reversal: it **breaks a combo from any direction**, even while you're being hit, for **2 Skill Count** (some launch-era guides listed 1 — current builds use 2). Unlike [[super-counter|Super Counter]] (free but ~2 frames and front-only), Revenge Counter is a guaranteed get-off-me that costs resources. Countering a Revenge Counter is a **Super Z-Counter**, whose skill cost can be shaved by 1 via the "Secret to Counters" item. See [[revenge-counter]] (mechanic page) and [[skill-count]].
