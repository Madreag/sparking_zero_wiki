**Sparking! Mode** is the game's super-state. To enter it you need a **full ki gauge + at least 1 skill stock**, then keep charging to fill the separate Sparking gauge and spend **1 skill stock** to activate (or use [[instant-sparking|Instant Sparking]] for 4 stocks to skip the gauge). It grants access to your **Ultimate Blast**, makes dashes and blasts **ki-free**, gives **immunity to ki-blast stun**, and applies the **"Boosted" damage values (~x1.2-1.5)**. Since the **May 26, 2026** patch, **activating Sparking! lowers your defense for a window**, so it can no longer be popped as a safe panic button. See [[instant-sparking]].
