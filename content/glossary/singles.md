**Singles (Single Battle)** is the **1v1** mode and ranked queue. The **June 23, 2025** patch (locked in cross-platform by **May 26, 2026**) made it a distinct game from [[dp|DP Battle]]: it **removed DP damage scaling** and **standardized all characters' HP**, so a character's DP cost is **irrelevant** here — only **kit quality** matters. That's why cheap DP-value tanks like **Yajirobe and Master Roshi are bad in Singles** (their tankiness edge is gone), while raw-kit fusions dominate. It also **removed heal-on-transform** (now a permanent attack buff). See the Singles half of [[current-meta-tier-list]].
