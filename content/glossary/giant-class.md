**Giant-Class** characters (Cell Max, Anilaza, Great Ape Vegeta/Baby, Hirudegarn, Lord Slug Giant, Orange Piccolo Giant, Giant Gomah, Fusion Android 13) have an **oversized hitbox**, **super armor on many moves**, and **grab/throw immunity** (a giant-stagger value instead). Their [[z-burst-dash|Z-Burst Dash]] costs a full **2 ki bars** (not 0.75). The **May 26, 2026** patch is the definitive nerf: giants now do **less rush/smash damage**, have **more whiff recovery**, **take more melee damage**, back-dash slower, and can be **damaged + knocked back by normal charge Blasts**. The giant-wall meta is broken — see [[cheese-tech-status-board]].
