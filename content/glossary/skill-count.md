**Skill Count** (a.k.a. **Skill Stock**) is the gauge that pays for Blast Skills, transformations, [[revenge-counter|Revenge Counters]], blast [[perception|Perception]], and [[instant-sparking|Instant Sparking]]. Max stock is **character-specific — typically 4-5** (Cell/Piccolo 5; many 4; Yajirobe raised **5 → 6**). It auto-regenerates at **~1 stock per ~14 seconds (~4.2/minute)**, a rate **slightly reduced** by the May 26 2026 patch. **Most characters start at 0 stocks**; Androids start **+1**, and ki-drain Androids (19, Dr. Gero) start **+2**. Spending stocks on defense means you can't transform — that tradeoff drives high-level play. See [[skill-count]] (mechanic page).
