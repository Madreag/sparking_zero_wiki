**Skill Count** (a.k.a. **Skill Stock**) is the gauge that pays for Blast Skills, transformations, [[revenge-counter|Revenge Counters]], blast [[perception|Perception]], and [[instant-sparking|Instant Sparking]]. Max stock is **character-specific — 4 or 6** (Babidi the lone **7**); **no fighter has 5** (Cell and Piccolo are **6**; many **4**; Yajirobe raised to **6**). It auto-regenerates at **~1 stock per ~14 seconds (~4.2/minute)**, a rate **slightly reduced** by the May 26 2026 patch. **Most characters start at 0 stocks**; Androids start **+1** — including the ki-drain pair **Android 19** and **Dr. Gero** (datamined `initialSkillStock` = **1**, **not +2**). Spending stocks on defense means you can't transform — that tradeoff drives high-level play. See [[skill-count]] (mechanic page).
