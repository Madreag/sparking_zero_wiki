**Super Armor** lets a character **absorb hits without flinching** while an armored move is active — core to heavies and [[giant-class|giants]]. The **"Power Body"** item grants **back-armor that takes a community-measured ~4-5 Smash/Rush-Chain hits to flinch**, in exchange for **-12% defense**. Armored moves take slightly reduced damage (a Rush-5 deals ~2,289 into armor vs ~2,460 normally, ~7% less). Heavies like **Broly, Recoome, Nappa, and Bojack** have armored moves natively rather than via a global tag. Armor is beaten by throws, [[unblockable|unblockable]] hits, and chip-then-punish.
