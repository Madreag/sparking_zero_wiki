**Vanishing Assault** is the teleport-strike approach: you warp directly to the opponent and land a melee hit. Its ki cost is **modest** — the "Dragon Assault" item reduces it by **16.5%**, which implies a small base cost. It's the offensive half of the vanish game: a defending player answers a Vanishing Assault with a [[z-counter|Z-Counter]], which (if both keep vanishing) escalates into a [[vanish-war|Vanish War]]. The Jan 2026 patch made it easier for **tall characters to land follow-ups on grounded foes**.
