**Sonic Sway** is triggered by a **frame-perfect Super Perception** against an opponent's **Rush** — instead of a normal parry, you **sway out, counter, and drain the opponent's ki**. It is the highest-skill defensive option in the game: nailing the perfect-timing window on a [[perception|Super Perception]] converts a defensive read into both a counter and a **ki swing** in your favor. See [[sonic-sway]] (mechanic page) and [[perception]].
