**High-Speed Dragon Dash** (hold **R2+X / RT+A**) is the fastest sustained movement option, roughly **50% faster than** a [[z-burst-dash|Z-Burst Dash]]. Added in the **Dec 11, 2024** update, it had its ki cost **"significantly reduced"** in the Jan 20 2025 patch and was tuned across later patches to **guarantee you reach the opponent's front** even if they manipulate their trajectory, with smoother Rush-Attack connects and reduced Smash charge time. It's a primary tool for closing on zoners and descending on a Z-Burst.
