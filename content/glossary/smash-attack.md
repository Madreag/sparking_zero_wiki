**Smash Attack** is the heavy melee hit (**△**) that **launches or wall-splats** the opponent, opening combo extensions and Chase follow-ups. The datamine puts a **full basic string at ~2,275 melee damage** and a **5-hit rush at ~2,460** (high-ATK bodies like Hit scale up to ~3,283). It's the launcher that turns a [[rush-chain|Rush Chain]] into a bigger combo, often into a [[lightning-attack|Lightning Attack]] pursuit. Smash Ki Blast variants were repeatedly nerfed (UI Goku cooldowns, Gogeta SSGSS homing) across 2025-2026 patches.
