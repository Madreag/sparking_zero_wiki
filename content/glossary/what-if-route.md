A **What-If Route** (in-game "Sparking Episode") is a **branching alternate storyline** in Episode Battle that diverges from canon. Routes unlock one of two ways: **(a) a dialogue choice** that alters the story, or **(b) a harder secondary win condition** — typically "defeat X within the time limit," "win taking minimal damage," or "win without transforming / without the Spirit Bomb." There are **12 major what-if routes** (e.g. Goku's "Side by Side," Vegeta's "Number One Spot") plus **~35 minor alternate stages**. The alternate conditions are deliberately **harder than the default clear**; a failed-Episode [[shenron-wish|Shenron wish]] grants a **Dragon Orb** to ease one. Full trigger table in [[episode-battle-completion]].
