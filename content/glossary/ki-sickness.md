**Ki Sickness** (Ki Exhaustion) is the punished state when your **ki gauge drains to empty and turns red** — you **cannot move** and most actions are locked until the gauge recovers. With only **5 ki bars** to work with, over-spending on dashes, [[z-counter|vanishes]] (**≈half a ki bar each**), or blasts can leave you exhausted and helpless. The Jan 2026 patch formalized an **action lock during ki depletion** (certain counters disabled while out of ki, with the mash-UI shown correctly). This is why winning a [[vanish-war|Vanish War]] depends on **having more ki** than your opponent.
