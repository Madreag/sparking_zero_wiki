**World Library** is the online hub where players **upload and download user-created Custom Battles**, and where you can **copy dev-authored Bonus Battles** into your own creations. It **requires the latest patch** to access. Custom Battle is the create/play/share mode exclusive to Sparking! ZERO, with a full trigger → effect logic editor; once you clear a **Bonus Battle**, its effects unlock for copying. Clearing a shared **"special battle"** pays roughly **10,000 Zeni** (community-cited, label-ambiguous). See [[zeni-economy-guide]] for the broader economy.
