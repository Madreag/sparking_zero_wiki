**Z-Burst Dash** is the fast approach dash that loops around the opponent. It costs **~0.75 ki** for a normal character but a steep **2 full ki bars** for a [[giant-class|giant]]. The **Jan 20 2025** patch cut the non-giant cost to 0.75, and the **May 26, 2026** patch reworked it so the **initial cost is lower but scales with travel distance** — short hops are cheap, full-screen dashes are expensive. It's countered by Dragon-Dash descend, switching just before the hit, or Instant Transmission. See [[dragon-dash]].
