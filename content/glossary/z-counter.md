A **Z-Counter** is the vanish reversal: press **R1/RB just before** an opponent's [[vanishing-assault|Vanishing Assault]] connects to teleport behind them and turn the attack around. It costs **~0.5 ki bars** per vanish (community-measured; the cost is not in the parameter tables), and each successive vanish is **faster and pricier**. Chaining Z-Counters with your opponent creates a [[vanish-war|Vanish War]] that triggers a **QTE/Impact clash after 4 consecutive vanishes** — and whoever holds **more ki wins**. The Dec 11 2024 patch shortened its input window and reduced the skill stock you gain from it. See [[vanish-z-counter]].
