**Perception** (hold guard / ○ / B) is the reactive parry. Against **melee** it's **ki-only**, but parrying a **blast** (like an incoming Kamehameha) costs **2 Skill Count**. The **Apr 21, 2025** nerf — re-locked cross-platform on **May 26, 2026** — made it harder to spam: it **cannot be used during guard stun**, its **Ki cost went up**, and the **Smash/Rush-Chain follow-up cost rose 1 → 2 stocks** (follow-ups are now chargeable, with a wider window). A frame-perfect **Super Perception** against a Rush triggers [[sonic-sway|Sonic Sway]], and Perception **cancels an active** [[afterimage-strike|Afterimage Strike]]. See [[perception]] (mechanic page).
