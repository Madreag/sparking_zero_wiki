**Versus** (the in-game **Battle** menu) is the **offline exhibition** mode for **1–2 local players**. It splits into two sub-modes:

| Sub-mode | Format |
|---|---|
| **Single Battle** | 1v1 (one character each) |
| **DP Battle** | Team battle — build under a **Destruction Points** budget |

## DP Battle (offline)

- **Default budget:** **15 DP**; team of **up to 5 characters**, each usable once.
- **Total now selectable:** **10 / 15 / 20 DP** (added across 2025–2026; the May 26 2026 update made the setting **persist**). This offline toggle is the source of one of the numbers that conflicts with the ranked queue — the full reconciliation lives on [[dp-system]].
- Example DP costs: Goku (Z-Mid) = 5 DP; Ultra Instinct Goku = 9 DP.

## Difficulty & farming

- **CPU difficulty** ladder gained **"Ultra"** (above "Super") in the **Jan 2026** patch.
- A quick CPU win pays **~1,000 Zeni** (community farm figure).

Online competitive versions of these formats: [[ranked-match]] (ranked) and [[player-match]] (casual). Team-format details: [[dp-system]]. Training: [[training]].
