**World Tournament** runs **bracket tournaments for up to 8 fighters**, **online or offline**. You can build a **custom bracket** or play **iconic series presets** — the **Tenkaichi Budokai**, **Tournament of Power**, **Cell Games**, **Tournament of Destroyers**, and more — each with its own stage / win-condition / roster ruleset.

## Rule toggles

| Toggle | Options |
|---|---|
| **HP recovery between matches** | **100% / 20% / 0%** |
| **Flying** | on / off |
| **Lose if out of bounds** | on / off |
| **Random fighter** assignment | on / off |
| **DP total** (DP-format brackets) | **10 / 15 / 20** (persists since May 26 2026) |

> The "**Random fighter**" toggle assigns each player a random character instead of letting them pick — combined with iconic presets, this is the community-referred "random" ruleset. Dragon Ball *collection* (Shenron wishes) is **not** tied to ranked PvP.

## Character unlocks (iconic presets)

| Character | Unlock |
|---|---|
| **Goku (Teen)** | Clear World Tournament 1st time |
| **Super Gogeta (Z)** | Clear World Tournament 1st time |
| **Frost** | Win Tournament of Destroyers |
| **Anilaza** | Win Tournament of Power |
