**Training** is the single-player **practice** mode. It splits into **Battle Training** (guided command/tutorial training) and **Free Practice**.

## Details

- **DP-format practice** supports the **10 / 15 / 20 DP** toggle, persistent since the **May 26 2026** update (see [[dp-system]]).
- **Challenge hook:** completing **Battle Training 5×** feeds a **Shenron Dragon Ball challenge** (Whis's-Wishes-style list).

To practice specific mechanics, see the mechanics pages (e.g. [[super-counter]], [[vanish-z-counter]]). Offline matches: [[versus]].
