**Player Match** is the **casual, unranked online** mode — host-created rooms with **custom rules** and **no rank impact** (it does not touch your [[ranked-match\|Ranked]] standing).

## Rules

- **Custom room rules** set by the host.
- If the room runs the **DP Battle** format, the **DP total** is selectable at **10 / 15 / 20** and the setting **persists** (since the **May 26 2026** update). See [[dp-system]].
- **Rewards:** Zeni for participation (casual). Exact per-match amount is **unconfirmed** (research/01 §Gaps).

For the competitive ladder instead, see [[ranked-match]]; for bracket events, [[world-tournament]]; for offline play, [[versus]].
