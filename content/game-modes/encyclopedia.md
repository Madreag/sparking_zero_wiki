## Dragon Ball wishes (alt unlock path)

Collecting Dragon Balls in battle summons one of three dragons to wish for characters, costumes, titles, ability items, or **Zeni**:

| Wish: "I want more Zeni" | Shenron | Porunga | Super Shenron |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Zeni granted** | **300,000** | **120,000** (×3 wishes per summon) | **1,000,000** |

Other wishes: raise Player Level, more titles (up to 11 options), new outfits (4 options), ability items (6 options), unlock characters (Goku Black / Fused Zamasu — Super Shenron only), or complete an Episode Battle (Shenron, after a failure → 1 Dragon Orb).
