**Ranked Match** is the **online competitive ladder** (2-player PvP). It runs **two queues** — **Single Battle** and **DP Battle** — and spans **26 ranks**, from **D5 (lowest)** up to **Rank Z (top)**.

## Ranks & queues

- **26 ranks**, tier bands **D → C → B → A → S → Z**, each with sub-divisions (e.g. **B1 / B3 / B5**, **C5**, **D5**) used in the reset mapping.
- **Single Battle queue:** 1v1; since May 26 2026, Singles uses **standardized/equal HP** and **no DP damage scaling** (see [[dp-system]]).
- **DP Battle queue:** team format at **15 DP** (meta research: **3-character team** standard — but see the conflict on [[dp-system]]). **Auto-reflect locked to DP7+** in Sparking! Mode.

## Seasons (official, 25 May 2026 notice)

| Event | When |
|---|---|
| **Season 0 ends** | **June 30, 2026, 08:00 UTC** |
| **Season 1 starts** | **July 1, 2026, 08:00 UTC** (runs **~3 months**) |

Final rank → cosmetic **title** reward. As of June 2026 the ladder is still labeled **"Season 0"**.

## Seasonal soft reset (your new starting rank)

| End-of-season rank | Resets to |
|---|---|
| **Z** | **B1** |
| **S** | **B3** |
| **A** | **B5** |
| **B** | **C5** |
| **C** | **D5** |
| **D** | **D5** |

## Win streaks & tracking

- **Win-streak bonus:** up to **2×** rank-point gains on streaks.
- Player Card tracks (since Jan 2026): **Longest Ranked Single Battle Win Streak** and **Longest Ranked DP Battle Win Streak** (season + lifetime).

> **Gaps:** published rank **distribution %** (how many players sit at each rank) is **not** available in official or aggregator sources as of June 10 2026. Per-match **Zeni** reward amount is also unconfirmed.
