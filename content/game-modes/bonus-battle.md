**Bonus Battles** are the **developer-authored Custom Battles** (preset characters, dialogue, win conditions). They **double as character-unlock content** and pay **tiered first-clear Zeni** by difficulty class. Total listed ≈ **37** (plus "Battles Under Certain Conditions" variants). They live under the **"Ultimate Battle"** menu alongside [[custom-battle\|Custom Battle]].

## Difficulty classes & first-clear Zeni

| Difficulty class | Battle count | Zeni per first clear |
|---|---|---|
| **Low-Class Warrior** | 10 | **20,000** |
| **High-Class Warrior** | 14 | **25,000** |
| **Elite Saiyan Warrior** | 10 | **30,000** |
| **Legendary Super Saiyan** | 3 | **35,000** |

**Player EXP** is always included on top of the Zeni.

## Unlocks & chaining

- Bonus Battles unlock specific roster members — e.g. **Bardock** ("Zamasu's Greatest Mistake"), **Turles** ("Avenger Returns"), **Majin Buu (Evil)** (all "To Be the Best" battles), **Frieza Force Soldier** ("Welcome to Frieza Force").
- Some **chain-unlock** others — e.g. *Hand over Kakarot!* unlocks *Who are you Callin' Number One?!*.
- The [[hero-of-justice-pack\|Hero of Justice Pack]] DLC added 3 Bonus Battles ("Outshine Grandfather", "Cell Max Games are underway", "Unleash Beast power!").
- After clearing a Bonus Battle, its **used effects unlock for copying** in [[custom-battle\|Custom Battle]] (Copy Edit).
