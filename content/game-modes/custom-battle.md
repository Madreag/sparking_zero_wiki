**Custom Battle** is Sparking! ZERO's **exclusive user-generated-content mode** — create, play and share scripted scenarios with pre/post-battle cutscenes, custom effects and item rules. Since the **Jan 2026 patch** it lives under the **"Ultimate Battle"** section of the main menu.

## Three creation modes

1. **Simple Edit** — guided/simplified, has its own tutorial.
2. **Normal Edit** — full editor; default starting scenario is **Goku vs Frieza on Namek**.
3. **Copy Edit** — duplicate an existing battle (your own OR a developer **Bonus Battle**) and tweak everything.

## Editable parameters (Normal Edit)

- Battle Character roster + **guest characters** (cutscene-only appearances).
- Per-participant: stats, transformations, equipped items, **CPU level** (max raised to **21** in Jan 2026; was lower at launch).
- **Trigger → Effect logic:** e.g. `IF Time Elapsed ≥ 60s THEN force active character to switch`. One trigger can chain **multiple effects** (force switch + stat boost + force into Sparking! Mode).
- **Special Match Settings / Conditional Win** effects: "Defeat a specific character", "Time Elapsed", etc.
- **3D scene editor:** poses, facial expressions, camera, intro/outro cuts (within the chosen scenario's bounds).
- After clearing a **Bonus Battle**, its used effects unlock for copying into your own creations.

## Sharing & rewards

- **World Library** (online; requires latest patch) — upload/download community battles.
- **Completion Zeni:** community-cited **~10,000** for shared/special battles (label is ambiguous across sources — research/01 §Gaps).

Developer-made battles are documented separately as [[bonus-battle\|Bonus Battles]] (tiered Zeni payouts). Related: [[episode-battle]], [[versus]].
