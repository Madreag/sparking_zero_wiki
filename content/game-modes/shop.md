**Shop** is the **Zeni spend hub** — characters, costumes, accessories, BGM, and **ability/strategy items**. Stock unlocks as your **Player Level** rises (cap **20**; at L20 effectively the full shop is purchasable).

## Ability Item price tiers (Zeni)

| Tier | Price | Example items |
|---|---|---|
| **Tier 1** | **15,000** | Melee Charge 1, Ki Blast Charge 1, Combo Master, Super Transformation, Sparking! Plus, Latent Power Unleashed 1 |
| **Tier 2** | **45,000** | King Kai Training, Rush Attack Boost 2, Energy Saver 2, Blast Attack Boost 2 (+30 more) |
| **Tier 3** | **150,000** | God of Destruction Training, Rush Attack Boost 3, The Secret to Counters, The Secret to Dragon Dashes, God Ki (+20 more) |

> The full **datamined** ability-item price table lives in the data collection at [[equippowerup]] (86 entries).

## Characters & BGM

| Category | Count | Price each | Total |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Characters** (shop-buyable bases) | **47** | **30,000 – 120,000** | ≈ **3,000,000 Zeni** for the whole roster |
| **BGM tracks** | **52** | **90,000** | ≈ **4,680,000 Zeni** |

- **Buying a base character unlocks ALL its variants/transformations/fusions** — you never pay per form.
- The 47 can alternatively be earned free via [[episode-battle]], [[bonus-battle]], [[world-tournament]], or Dragon Ball wishes.
- **Goku (Mini)** is the **one** base-roster character not buyable in-game — it's the [[preorder-pack\|Pre-Order Pack]] bonus.
