Smash and the Rush Chain are the **core offense**. The **Rush Chain** is `□ (×1–4 max) + △` — a basic string that can be cut short or run long — and the **Smash** is the heavy attack that launches or wall-bounces to start a damage combo.

## The numbers

- **Rush Chain = □ ×1–4 then △.** A longer chain does **more raw damage** but has two costs: it opens **more escape windows** for the opponent ([[super-counter|Super Counter]], [[vanish-z-counter|Vanish]], [[perception|Perception]] all get more chances), and — when you hit the opponent **from behind** — it grants the **victim more skill-stock recovery**. So mashing the full string actively **funds the defender's** [[skill-count|skill stocks]].
- **Rush 5-hit damage: ~2,460**, dropping to **~2,289 into [[super-armor|armor]]** (≈7% less). See [[health-and-damage|health & damage]] for the full melee table.
- **Optimal = short.** Between combo scaling (long strings lose damage) and the skill-stock-to-victim cost, the high-level standard is a **short** rush chain into a Smash launcher and a [[lightning-attack-pursuit|pursuit]] conversion.

## Beats auto-dodge (since May 26, 2026)

- As of the **May 26, 2026** free update, the **rush chain beats UI Goku / [[whis|Whis]] auto-dodge** — auto-evasion skills no longer cleanly escape a rush chain. This closed a long-standing hole where the dodge-heavy top tier (UI Goku, Whis, [[afterimage-strike|Afterimage]] users) could simply auto-dodge their way out of pressure. The same patch made general unblockable **Rush Blasts** evadable *with* auto-evasion skills (the inverse tweak on the blast side).

## Interactions

- The **Apr 21, 2025** patch fixed a hole where the opponent could **guard between** a Rush-In and the following Rush Attacks, tightening the pressure.
- Smash launches feed the [[lightning-attack-pursuit|Lightning Attack pursuit]] (capped at 6 chases), which is the standard route from a launcher to a finisher.
- Rush pressure is answered by the full defensive suite: [[super-counter|Super Counter]] (free, front), [[vanish-z-counter|Vanish]] (ki), [[revenge-counter|Revenge Counter]] (2 stocks, any direction), and [[perception|Perception]] / [[sonic-sway|Sonic Sway]] (reads).
