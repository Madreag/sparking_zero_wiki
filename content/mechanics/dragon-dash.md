Dragon Dash is the **fast straight-line approach**. The **High-Speed Dragon Dash** (hold **R2 + ✕ / RT + A**) flies you directly at the locked-on opponent — the quickest way to close neutral distance.

## The numbers

- **Speed: ~50% faster than [[z-burst-dash|Z-Burst Dash]]** (community comparison after the Jan 2025 buff). Where Z-Burst arcs *around* the opponent, Dragon Dash drives straight in.
- **Ki cost: "significantly reduced"** by the Jan 20, 2025 patch — but Bandai gives **no number**. The exact current ki cost is **not published and not community-measured** into a hard figure, and it has **not been re-measured since May 26, 2026**.
- **Historical baseline:** a widely-repeated community estimate put launch-era Dragon Dash at **~2.5 ki bars**. That figure is **pre-Jan-2025 and stale** — the Jan 2025 cut and later ki re-tunings make it unreliable for the current build; cite it only as a historical reference.
- **Front-guarantee (Apr 21, 2025):** the dash now **reaches the opponent's front** even if they manipulate their trajectory to dodge, and connects into Rush Attacks more easily.

## Interactions

- On arrival the opponent can [[vanish-z-counter|Vanish]], [[perception|Perception]], or [[super-counter|Super Counter]] your follow-up — Dragon Dash gets you *there* but doesn't make the hit safe.
- Since **Jan 26, 2026**, **Solar Flare** no longer ends when you approach through Dragon Dash or [[z-burst-dash|Z-Burst Dash]], closing a former hard-counter to the blind.
- Starting a Dragon Dash from a Short Dash has **reduced recovery** (Apr 21, 2025), making the short-dash → Dragon Dash cancel a smooth approach tool.
- Compare the movement options: **Dragon Dash** = straight + fastest; **[[z-burst-dash|Z-Burst Dash]]** = around the opponent, distance-scaled ki; **[[vanishing-assault|Vanishing Assault]]** = teleport into pressure.
