Perception is the active parry: **hold Perception (○/B)** to read an incoming attack, deflect it on a successful read, and release into a Smash or Rush-Chain follow-up. **Super Perception** is the stronger, longer-buff version that also nicks the opponent's ki on a blast deflect.

## The numbers

- **Melee Perception is cheap** — **ki only**, no skill stocks — which is why it was a launch-era staple. Reading a **blast** (a Kamehameha-class super) instead costs **2 skill stocks**.
- **Smash / Rush-Chain follow-up cost: 1 → 2 skill stocks.** The Apr 21, 2025 patch raised the stock requirement to release Perception into a Smash or Rush Chain from **1 to 2**, simultaneously **adding/raising the ki cost on activation** and **disabling Perception during guard stun**. The May 26, 2026 free update **re-locked this same change cross-platform**, raising ki cost again and confirming the 1→2 requirement and the guard-stun lockout on all platforms.
- **Ki on activation:** at launch Perception was free to start; **Jan 20, 2025** added a set ki cost on activation and lengthened recovery; **Apr 21, 2025** and **May 26, 2026** each increased that ki cost further. The exact ki figure is **not published in any official note** and has **not been community-measured into a hard number** since May 26, 2026 — the direction (up, repeatedly) is all that is documented.

## Interactions

- **Cancels [[afterimage-strike]]:** landing a Perception on an Afterimage Strike user ends their active dodge buff (since Dec 2024). Perception also cancels any auto-evasion skill if used during its activation (Apr 2025).
- **Frame-perfect Super Perception vs a Rush triggers [[sonic-sway|Sonic Sway]]** — the dodge-and-counter that also drains the attacker's ki.
- **Cannot be used during guard stun** since Apr 21, 2025, so you can't mash Perception to escape blockstun — that gap is what [[guard-and-chip|guarding]] now leaves open.
- Compared to the other defensive tools, Perception is the **read-based** option: cheaper than [[vanish-z-counter|Vanish]] vs melee but harder, and unlike [[super-counter|Super Counter]] it works on blasts (for 2 stocks) and feeds a damaging follow-up.
