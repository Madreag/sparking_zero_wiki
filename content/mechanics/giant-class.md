The **Giant** class covers the oversized bodies — [[great-ape-vegeta|Great Ape Vegeta]], [[orange-piccolo-giant-form|Orange Piccolo Giant Form]], Great Ape Baby, [[dr-wheelo|Dr. Wheelo]], and the like. Giants trade **mobility** for **bulk and armor**, and have been the single most-nerfed archetype in the game's history.

## The numbers

- **Z-Burst Dash costs 2 full ki bars** for a giant, versus **0.75** for a normal body — roughly a **3× mobility tax**. See [[z-burst-dash|Z-Burst Dash]].
- **Grab/throw-immune.** A giant cannot be [[throws|thrown]]; instead it has a **stagger threshold** (~**4–5** hits to flinch). Armor also reduces incoming melee per hit — a rush-5 that does ~**2,460** on a normal body did ~**2,289** into a giant (≈7% less) **before** the May 2026 melee-damage-taken increase.
- **HP:** most giants sit in the top **45,000** HP tier (Great Ape Vegeta, etc.) — see [[health-and-damage|health & damage]].

## Nerf history

Giants were brought down over three major passes:

- **Dec 11, 2024** — Great Ape Vegeta / [[dr-wheelo|Dr. Wheelo]] max **health reduced**; giant **Smash charge time up, power down**; giant **Z-Burst tackle made guardable**; chase-change damage from giants reduced. (A mild first dent.)
- **Apr 21, 2025** — the **biggest** pass: rush & smash **damage reduced**, **whiff recovery increased**, **damage taken from melee increased**, backward Short Dash slower. Giants now **deal less and take more**.
- **May 26, 2026** — the **definitive cross-platform lock** of the Apr 2025 nerf, plus standard **charge-type Blasts now damage + knock them back** even while the giant is in [[sparking-mode|Sparking! Mode]] (they used to whiff/nullify against giants — this was also folded in from Jun 23, 2025).

> The **~2,289 rush-5-into-giant** figure is a **pre-May-2026 datamine** number; the May 26, 2026 "damage taken from melee increased" change means the **current** effective melee-into-giant value is **higher and has not been re-datamined** since.

## Interactions

- The **2-bar Z-Burst** plus the slower back-dash make giants **easy to space and zone** — a deliberate weakness balancing their HP and armor.
- Standard charge-blasts and the [[smash-and-rush|rush chain]] are now reliable answers; the [[afterimage-strike|auto-dodge]] top tier doesn't help a giant, which has no evasion class.
