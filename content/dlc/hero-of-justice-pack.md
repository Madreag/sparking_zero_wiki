**Hero of Justice Pack** (DLC 1) — released **Jan 23, 2025** (Season Pass holders got **early access Jan 20**) at **$8.99**. It pulls **11 characters** from *Dragon Ball Super: Super Hero* (2022), filling roster slots **#183–193**, and immediately reshaped the meta: [[gohan-beast\|Gohan Beast]] (DP 9) became a top-tier carry and [[cell-max\|Cell Max]] anchored the early Giant meta.

> **Price conflict:** the official figure in PVE research is **$8.99**; the roster research lists a **$17.99** standalone (GG.deals). Both are recorded here; $8.99 is used as the page price.
