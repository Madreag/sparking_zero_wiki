**Anime Music Packs 1 & 2** — two **standalone music DLCs** dated **Oct 7, 2024** at **$14.99 each**. They add classic *Dragon Ball* anime opening/ending themes and BGM, usable as battle music in regular battles, **Custom Battles**, and more. **No characters.** Both sit **outside** the [[season-pass-1\|Season Pass]].

## Contents (datamined — DLC_003 / DLC_004)

| Pack | Tracks | Price | Note |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Anime Music Pack 1** | **11** classic anime OP/ED & BGM | $14.99 | usable as BGM in battles & Custom Battles |
| **Anime Music Pack 2** | **12** classic anime OP/ED & BGM | $14.99 | usable as BGM in battles & Custom Battles |

> These DLC tracks are **separate** from the **52** in-game shop BGM tracks (90,000 Zeni each; full shop-BGM unlock ≈ 4,680,000 Zeni — research/06 §3.2). See [[shop]], [[custom-battle]].
