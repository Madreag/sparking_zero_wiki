**Shallot (DRAGON BALL LEGENDS)** — the **free Season Pass bonus** character. Shallot is the original protagonist of the *Dragon Ball Legends* mobile game, brought in as **1 roster slot (#202)** at **DP 5** with **no transformations** in this port.

Distribution:
- **Free** with the **Season Pass**, **Deluxe**, and **Ultimate** editions.
- Cheap **standalone** purchase (~**$1.99** per research/02).
- This is the **4th "DLC pack"** referenced in the Season Pass marketing ("4 DLC Packs, 20+ characters"); the other three are the [[hero-of-justice-pack\|Hero of Justice Pack]] + the two DAIMA packs.

> **Date nuance:** "available now" across PS5/Xbox/PC in **2025** (research/06); roster research dates the PC/console drop **Jun 26, 2025**. The **Nintendo Switch / Switch 2** version received Shallot on **May 27, 2026** alongside the May 26 free update — and even there the Switch date conflicts (official tweet "May 27" vs EU site "June 27"; unreconciled, research/06 §Gaps). Page uses Jun 26, 2025 (PC/console).
