**Shallot (DRAGON BALL LEGENDS)** — the **free Season Pass bonus** character. Shallot is the original protagonist of the *Dragon Ball Legends* mobile game, brought in as **1 roster slot (#202)** at **DP 5** with **no transformations** in this port.

Distribution: **free** (so `type: free`, `priceUSD: null` at the data level).
- **Free** with the **Season Pass**, **Deluxe**, and **Ultimate** editions — the dominant way owners get Shallot.
- A cheap **standalone** purchase (~**$1.99** per research/02) exists where sold separately, but the headline distribution is free — hence the null page price (the $1.99 nuance is kept here, not in the frontmatter).
- This is the **4th "DLC pack"** referenced in the Season Pass marketing ("4 DLC Packs, 20+ characters"); the other three are the [[hero-of-justice-pack\|Hero of Justice Pack]] + the two DAIMA packs.

> **Date nuance:** "available now" across PS5/Xbox/PC in **2025** (research/06). The PC/console drop is dated **Jun 27, 2025** on the patch page ([[shallot-2025-06-27]]) and **Jun 26, 2025** in roster research — an off-by-one. The **Nintendo Switch / Switch 2** version received Shallot on **May 27, 2026** alongside the May 26 free update — and even there the Switch date conflicts (official tweet "May 27" vs EU site "June 27"; unreconciled, research/06 §Gaps). This page uses **Jun 27, 2025** to match the patch page.
