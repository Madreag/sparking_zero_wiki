**Season Pass 1** — the **$34.99** Season 1 bundle, sold from launch (**Oct 10, 2024**). It packages the three paid DLC packs plus the bonus Shallot character, advertised as **"4 DLC Packs, more than 20 playable characters."** Across those packs it delivered **26 roster slots**, raising the base **182** to the current live **208**.

> **Price source:** $34.99 is from IGN (research/01 §Gaps notes Bandai/PS/Nintendo product pages list contents but not the price). Included **free** in the **Deluxe** ($99.99) and **Ultimate** ($109.99) editions.
