**Jiren's campaign** is the **Universe Survival** arc, confined to **chapter 7**. **Unlock:** **beat Goku's saga** ([[episode-goku]]) — it is **not** available from the start. Each new mission pays **15,000 Zeni** on first clear; this page tracks **11 battles** including the "True Strength" Sparking Episode.

## Walkthrough & boss strategies

Jiren's whole campaign is a course in **fighting [[goku-super-ultra-instinct\|Ultra Instinct Goku]]**, who **auto-dodges close-range melee almost every time** — so the mash-rush plan that wins everywhere else is actively useless here. It culminates in **"The Defeated,"** the **single hardest Episode Battle in the game**.

### The Ultra Instinct Goku problem (Stages 5, t2, t4, t5)

UI Goku appears in nearly every late stage. He **slips your punches and kicks on reaction**, and **one of his ki attacks — let alone his Sparking super — erases a chunk of Jiren's health**. Melee-first does not work. The ranged plan:

- **Win at range with ki, not fists.** Charge ki to distance, then pressure with **ki blasts and supers**; UI's auto-dodge is a *melee* read, so ranged pressure is your reliable damage.
- **When he closes, hit-and-teleport.** Engage briefly, land the few melee hits he gives you, then **Instant Transmission behind him** and knock him across the map to re-open charging space. Don't get greedy in melee.
- **Bank [[sparking-mode\|Sparking]] before committing.** In Sparking your actions are ki-free and you ignore ki-blast stun — the safest window to actually land damage on him.
- **Heal with Meditation BEFORE the hard phase.** Jiren's **Meditation restores a full health bar** and needs **4 [[skill-count\|skill stock]]**; use it **at least twice** across the run so you enter each UI Goku phase near full. *Starting these fights at max health is the difference between winning and a death spiral.*

### "The Defeated" — the hardest fight in the mode (True Strength, Stage t4)

A **4-on-1**: you must beat **[[android-17-z\|Android 17]], [[golden-frieza\|Golden Frieza]], [[vegeta-super-super-saiyan-god-super-saiyan\|SSGSS Vegeta]], and [[goku-super-ultra-instinct\|Goku]]** in one battle — and **when Goku falls he powers up to FULL Ultra Instinct and recovers ALL of his health**, so you effectively beat him twice. It is the campaign's gauntlet and an endurance test:

- **Preserve health through the first three.** Use **[[perception]] and High-Speed Evasion** to dodge rather than trade against 17/Frieza/Vegeta — whatever HP you carry into the UI Goku phase is all you get for it.
- **Re-Meditate before Goku revives.** If you have skill stock when his first bar drops, heal — the full-power UI phase is unwinnable from low HP.
- **Peel and isolate** with [[z-burst-dash\|Z-Burst Dash]] so you're never sandwiched; **[[revenge-counter\|Revenge Counter]] (2 stock)** to escape a gang combo.
- **For the UI Goku phases, run the range + Instant-Transmission loop above** — and when totally pinned, **Jiren's "No Backing Down" skill (3 skill stock) snaps you into Sparking instantly** with damage/defense buffs to seize tempo.

### Full Power Jiren / base-Goku phases (main route)

In the main "Ultimate Climactic Battle" you fight as Jiren and may face Full Power Jiren or fight alongside [[golden-frieza\|Frieza]] and [[android-17-z\|Android 17]] (switch out a low-HP fighter to keep momentum):

- **Defense to make space:** when you can't establish offense, **Super [[perception\|Perception]] or High-Speed Evasion** to counter and knock the opponent away, then charge.
- **His Ultimate is unblockable + huge AoE** — during any enemy Sparking phase, **keep maximum distance**; you cannot guard it, only avoid it.

## Sparking episodes & 100% completion

Jiren has **one Sparking Episode** (*True Strength*) plus a minor alternate stage; 100% needs both, and they are **mutually exclusive in a single run**.

| Sparking Episode | Trigger | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **True Strength** | In *Saiyan Rampage*, choose **"Wait and See"** instead of fighting enraged [[kale\|Kale]]. | 5 stages incl. *The Defeated*; **"Saiyans Awaken" unlocks Goku (Super) UI -Sign-**. |

**Minor alternate stage:**

| Stage | Trigger | Unlocks |
|---|---|---|
| *Ultimate Climactic Battle* | **Win without** having taken the True Strength route | "Strength is Absolute" |

**Missables:** the route fork at *Saiyan Rampage* is the catch — taking **"Wait and See"** locks you into **True Strength**, while the **"Strength is Absolute"** alternate requires **winning the main *Ultimate Climactic Battle* WITHOUT** the True Strength route. The two cannot both be earned in one playthrough, so plan to **replay** the campaign from the start to capture the other side. **Goku (Super) Ultra Instinct -Sign-** is only obtainable via *Saiyans Awaken* on the True Strength route, so that route is mandatory for the character unlock.

**Notable unlocks:** **Goku (Super) Ultra Instinct -Sign-** — via "Saiyans Awaken" (True Strength route).
