**Piccolo's campaign** covers **chapters 1 through 4** (Saiyan → Android/Cell) and is **available from the start**. Each new mission pays **15,000 Zeni** on first clear; this page tracks **17 battles** including the Sparking Episode "A Mentor's Guidance."

> **Trigger note (research/06 §Gaps #2):** the **"A Mentor's Guidance"** unlock had two conflicting documented conditions. Game8's **per-campaign** page resolves it as **"defeat [[frieza-z-1st-form\|Frieza (1st Form)]] within 2 minutes in *Piccolo Returns to his Birthplace*"** — that is the trigger used below. The older "step into Goku's fight vs Android 19" wording came from the cross-campaign What-If index and appears to be a mislabel.

## Walkthrough & boss strategies

Piccolo's main line is short and mostly even (Nappa, Vegeta-Scouter, Frieza-line, Cell 1st Form). The difficulty is the **2-minute Frieza timer** that gates the Sparking Episode and the **[[perfect-cell\|Perfect Cell]]** fight that ends it.

### Stage 4 — Piccolo Returns to his Birthplace (the 2-minute Frieza gate)

To branch into **A Mentor's Guidance**, you must drop **[[frieza-z-1st-form\|Frieza (1st Form)]] in under 2 minutes**. 1st-Form Frieza is weak but the clock is tight if you fish for combos:

- **Bank ki before the bell.** Piccolo carries **5 max [[skill-count\|skill stock]]** and a strong kit; start charging immediately so you can pop [[sparking-mode\|Sparking]] within the first ~20 seconds.
- **Special Beam Cannon is your timer-killer.** Piccolo's Ultimate (Special Beam Cannon) reads **15,000 base / ~19,600 boosted** — a single boosted cast from Sparking removes a huge chunk of 1st-Form Frieza. Land it from a knockdown.
- **Short rush → △ → Light Grenade**, reset, repeat. Don't over-commit to long chains; the timer rewards bursts, not style.

### Stage 2 — The Saiyans Attack (Nappa quick-kill)

For the **Vegeta vs. Piccolo** alternate, beat **[[nappa\|Nappa]]** fast. He's a slow [[super-armor\|armored]] bruiser; space him, beam him with Special Beam Cannon / Light Grenade, and avoid trading punches into his armor.

### A Mentor's Guidance — Piccolo vs Perfect Cell finale

The 8-stage route ends on **[[perfect-cell\|Perfect Cell]]**, and Piccolo is genuinely under-statted against him: Cell **spams Super Kamehameha, interrupts your charge with ki blasts, and blocks/escapes Piccolo's rush chains** harder than most. The community-proven answer is a **specific move-spam**:

- **Spam Light Grenade (S1).** Cell's evasion AI is **measurably worse against Piccolo's Light Grenade** than against his melee — repeated Light Grenades land where combos get blocked. It's the single most reliable damage source in this matchup.
- **Charge ki in the air after a knockback** (Cell can't snipe you mid-fall), then convert banked ki straight into **Special Beam Cannon** on his recovery.
- **[[perception\|Perception]] his Super Kamehameha (2 stock)** when cornered; dash the telegraphed ones for free.
- **Keep strings short** — Cell escapes long rushes and you bleed [[skill-count\|skill stock]] to him on rear hits.

## Sparking episodes & 100% completion

Piccolo has **one Sparking Episode** plus a minor alternate stage; 100% requires both.

| Sparking Episode | Trigger | Length |
|---|---|---|
| **A Mentor's Guidance** | In *Piccolo Returns to his Birthplace*, defeat [[frieza-z-1st-form\|Frieza (1st Form)]] **within 2 minutes**. | 8 stages |

**Minor alternate stage:**

| Stage | Trigger | Unlocks |
|---|---|---|
| *The Saiyans Attack* | Defeat [[nappa\|Nappa]] in a short span of time | "Vegeta vs. Piccolo" |

**Missables:** the **2-minute Frieza** window is the only hard gate — miss it and you stay on the main line, so **replay** *Piccolo Returns to his Birthplace* with Sparking + Special Beam Cannon banked. Separately, playing **[[piccolo\|Piccolo]] 10×** (any mode counted) unlocks **Super Garlic Jr.** — a grind, not a battle, but part of full completion.

**Notable unlocks:** **Nail** ("Piccolo Returns to his Birthplace"), **Lord Slug** ("Piccolo vs. Gohan"), **Super Garlic Jr.** (play Piccolo 10×).
