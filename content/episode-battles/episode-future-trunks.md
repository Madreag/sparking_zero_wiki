**Future Trunks's campaign** covers **chapters 6 and 7** (Future Trunks arc → Universe Survival). **Unlock:** **beat Goku's saga** ([[episode-goku]]) — it is **not** available from the start. Each new mission pays **15,000 Zeni** on first clear; this page tracks **17 battles** across three Sparking Episodes.

## Walkthrough & boss strategies

Trunks's campaign is brutal up front: the **second** stage is already a 2-on-1 against **[[goku-black-super-saiyan-ros\|Goku Black (Rosé)]] + [[zamasu\|Zamasu]]**, the main line ends on **[[fused-zamasu-half-corrupted\|Half-Corrupted Fused Zamasu]]**, and the alternate route opens with a **3-on-1**. Trunks has a great tool for all of it: **"Power Up to the Very Limit"** instantly drops him into [[sparking-mode\|Sparking]].

### Stage 2 — Twisted World (Black + Zamasu duo, ~1:40 gate)

The **Shining Hope** trigger is the campaign's nastiest: defeat **[[zamasu\|Zamasu]]** with **[[goku-black-super-saiyan-ros\|Goku Black (Rosé)]] also on the field, in ~1:40**. GameRant ranks the Black+Zamasu duo among the hardest fights in the whole mode — both have **large health pools and strong defensive abilities**. Plan:

- **Use "Power Up to the Very Limit" turn-1** for instant Sparking (no gauge wait). You cannot afford the ~20-second charge against a 1:40 clock with two aggressors.
- **Hard-focus Zamasu.** The clock only cares about Zamasu; ignore Black except to peel. Two **Sparking rush rotations into Trunks's Ultimate (Heat Dome Attack / Final Hope Slash)** can melt Zamasu inside the window.
- **Whittle then super:** *"use Sparking combos to whittle health and unleash super moves to finish health bars efficiently"* — short rush, knockdown, super on recovery. Long combos scale down and let the off-target intervene.
- **[[vanish-z-counter\|Vanish-punish]] (~0.5 ki/vanish)** when they pressure you; bail the clash if you're behind on ki rather than losing the QTE.

### Stages 3–4 — Fused Zamasu / Half-Corrupted (the regen wall)

The main line ends on **[[fused-zamasu\|Fused Zamasu]]** → **[[fused-zamasu-half-corrupted\|Half-Corrupted]]**. His **"Immortal Body" regenerates an estimated ~20% of max HP** *(community estimate; enemy HP/regen are not datamined)*, so a slow poke war loses — you must **out-burst the heal**:

- **Aggression in [[sparking-mode\|Sparking]]:** *"play aggressively, enter Sparking Mode, rush down with endless rush combos followed by an ultimate"* — two 20+-hit rotations into an Ultimate erase a bar faster than he restores it.
- **Dodge/[[perception\|Perception]] his energy supers** — Holy Wrath, Blades of Judgment, and the Lightning of Absolution Ultimate **stagger your bar**; don't tank them. Perception costs **2 skill stock** per blast deflect.
- **[[sonic-sway\|Sonic Sway]] / [[vanish-z-counter\|Vanishes]] for openings** when he counterattacks; a frame-perfect Super Perception vs his rush flips into Sonic Sway (dodge + counter + drains his ki).

### Inherited Pride — the 3-on-1 opener and the Jiren finales

Choosing "stay in the present" opens **Inherited Pride**, which starts with **"To Become the Strongest in the Universe..."** — a **3-on-1** vs **[[vegeta-z-end\|Vegeta]] + [[piccolo\|Piccolo]] + [[gohan-adult\|Gohan]]** (all SSJ where applicable), required **quickly**. GameRant flags this as a wall: three enemies, strict clock.

- **Instant Sparking turn-1**, then **AoE and isolate.** Peel to the arena edge with [[z-burst-dash\|Z-Burst Dash]] so only one hero reaches you; kill them one at a time.
- **[[revenge-counter\|Revenge Counter]] (2 stock)** every time two of them chain you — it's your only clean escape from a cross-up combo.
- Both route finales (**Embarking Toward Tomorrow** and **Inherited Pride**) end on **[[jiren\|Jiren]]** — charge → short rush → interrupt your own chain with a ki blast so he can't escape-vanish → Ultimate on knockback; **never** stand in his unblockable Sparking Ultimate (large AoE). Full Jiren tactics on the [[episode-jiren\|Jiren page]].

## Sparking episodes & 100% completion

Future Trunks has **three Sparking Episodes** (the most of any campaign), gated by a fast-kill timer and a branching story choice; 100% needs all three plus the main line.

| Sparking Episode | Trigger | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Shining Hope** | In *Twisted World*, defeat [[zamasu\|Zamasu]] **quickly (~1:40)** with Black also present. | Hardest trigger in the campaign. |
| **Inherited Pride** | In *Trunks's Choice*, **stay in the present**, then in *Father or Teamwork?* **go with Vegeta**. | Opens with the 3-on-1; unlocks **Kale**. |
| **Embarking Toward Tomorrow** | On the Inherited-Pride path, in *Father or Teamwork?* **go with Gohan**. | Ends on a [[jiren\|Jiren]] fight. |

**Missables:** *Father or Teamwork?* is a **one-or-the-other split** — "go with Gohan" (Embarking Toward Tomorrow) and "go with Vegeta" (Inherited Pride) cannot both happen in one run, so 100% requires **replaying** to take the other branch (the Vegeta branch is where **Kale** unlocks). The **~1:40 Shining Hope** window is the most failure-prone timed gate; replay *Twisted World* with instant-Sparking banked if you miss it.

**Notable unlocks:** **Tapion** ("Final Battle with Zamasu"), **Kale** ("Vegeta's Promise", go-with-Vegeta branch).
