**Frieza's campaign** covers **chapters 2, 3, 5 and 7** (Namek → Android/Cell → Frieza's Resurrection → Universe Survival) and is **available from the start**. Each new mission pays **15,000 Zeni** on first clear; this page tracks **17 battles** including the Sparking Episode "Frieza Force Joins the Fray."

> This campaign also ties to the notable **"Frieza Force Joins the Fray"** achievement (research/06 §5), earned by unlocking that Sparking Episode.

## Walkthrough & boss strategies

Frieza's campaign is unusual: several stages are **4-on-1 handicap melees** (you, alone, vs four Z-fighters), which are the real difficulty rather than any single boss. The Sparking Episode is mostly even Tournament-of-Power duels.

### Stages 1–2 — Crushing Worthless Insects / A Blast from the Past (4-on-1)

Both stages put one Frieza against **four opponents at once** (Krillin, Kid Gohan, Piccolo, + Vegeta-Scouter or Goku). Being ganged up on, you cannot afford to get combo-locked. Survival rules:

- **Never stand in the middle.** Use [[z-burst-dash\|Z-Burst Dash]] (~0.75 ki) to peel to the **edge** so only one enemy can reach you at a time; fighting them in single file is the whole game.
- **[[revenge-counter\|Revenge Counter]] (R3, 2 stock) is your panic button** when two of them chain you — it breaks a combo from any direction, even mid-hit. Bank stock for it.
- **AoE supers thin the pack:** Frieza's Death Ball / Nova Strike and Explosive-Wave-type clears hit multiple targets; pop [[sparking-mode\|Sparking]] (ki-free actions) and spread damage rather than tunnelling one enemy.
- **Quick-clear what-if ("Immortal Body"):** defeat all four **fast** — go straight to Sparking, AoE, and Ultimate; don't duel them one at a time.

### Stage 3 — Legendary Super Saiyan (the no-transform Goku kill)

For **"Fall of the Saiyans,"** you must beat **[[goku-z-mid-super-saiyan\|SSJ Goku]] WITHOUT transforming** Frieza. That means fighting in a weaker form, so lean on the engine, not raw stats:

- **[[super-counter\|Super Counter]] (↑+□) costs nothing** — a ~2-frame free reversal. Against AI Goku's rush, counter the launcher and you flip his pressure for free without needing a stronger form.
- **Spend ki on supers, not transforming.** Death Beam / Death Ball chip him down; keep strings short so scaling doesn't gut your damage.
- **[[perception\|Perception]] his Kamehameha (2 stock)** and punish the recovery.

### Frieza Revived (the fast-win gate) + revival branches

To open **Frieza Force Joins the Fray**, beat **[[goku-super\|Goku]] and [[vegeta-super\|Vegeta]] (Super)** *quickly* in "Frieza Revived." This is a 2-on-1 against strong duelists — use **Golden Frieza in Sparking**, focus one target down with short rush → Ultimate, switch targets, and avoid long vanishing wars you might lose on ki. Once unlocked, **Frieza's Philosophy** lets you revive **Ginyu Force / Cooler / Dodoria+Zarbon / King Cold** — each opens a different fight ([[ribrianne\|Ribrianne]] / [[hit\|Hit]] / [[bergamo\|Bergamo]]+[[frost\|Frost]] / [[caulifla\|Caulifla]]+[[kale\|Kale]]). [[hit\|Hit]] (Cooler branch) is the spiciest — he has a strong counter game, so **don't throw predictable rushes**; bait, [[vanish-z-counter\|vanish]], and punish.

## Sparking episodes & 100% completion

Frieza has **one Sparking Episode** with **four mutually-exclusive revival branches**, plus minor alternate stages; 100% requires clearing **all four branches** (separate playthroughs/replays) and the alternate stages.

| Sparking Episode | Trigger | Branches |
|---|---|---|
| **Frieza Force Joins the Fray** | In *Frieza Revived*, defeat Goku & Vegeta (Super) **quickly**. | In *Frieza's Philosophy*, choose **Ginyu Force / Cooler / Dodoria+Zarbon / King Cold** — one branch per run. |

**Minor alternate stages:**

| Stage | Trigger | Unlocks |
|---|---|---|
| *A Blast from the Past* | Defeat all four enemies **quickly** | "Immortal Body" |
| *Legendary Super Saiyan* | Defeat Goku **without transforming** | "Fall of the Saiyans" |

**Missables:** the **four revival branches share one choice node** — you can only take one per playthrough, so reaching 100% means **replaying *Frieza's Philosophy* four times** to see Ginyu Force, Cooler, Dodoria+Zarbon and King Cold. The "Immortal Body" and "Fall of the Saiyans" what-ifs also each need their own clean replay if you missed the timer/no-transform condition first time.

**Notable unlocks:** **Mecha Frieza** ("Frieza Descends on Earth"), **King Cold** ("Parent-Child Tournament of Power" — King Cold branch), **Dyspo** ("Virtuous Warrior, Virtuous Villain").
