**Gohan's campaign** covers **chapters 3 through 7** (Android/Cell → Universe Survival) and is **available from the start**. Each new mission pays **15,000 Zeni** on first clear; this page tracks **22 battles** including the Sparking Episode "The Strongest Warrior."

## Walkthrough & boss strategies

Gohan's campaign is front-loaded: it **opens against [[perfect-cell\|Perfect Cell]]** while you are still learning the engine, and the what-if route ends against **[[fused-zamasu\|Fused Zamasu]]**. Those two bosses are the whole difficulty.

### Stages 2–4 — Gohan (SSJ2) vs Perfect Cell

[[perfect-cell\|Perfect Cell]] is the early Gohan wall and one of the most-complained-about PvE bosses: he **spams Super Kamehameha, shoots ki blasts to interrupt your charge, and reliably blocks/escapes long rush chains**. SSJ2 Teen Gohan hits hard but is squishy, so a couple of mistakes end the run. Win it on tempo:

- **Charge ki mid-air after a knockback.** Launch Cell, and during his fall he can't shoot — that is your safe **R2 charge** to bank [[sparking-mode\|Sparking]]. Charging on the ground gets sniped.
- **Short combos only.** Cell escapes long strings; `□×4 → △ → Super` then reset. Long rush chains also feed him [[skill-count\|skill stock]] on rear hits and scale your damage down hard — a **raw super beats a long-combo-into-super**.
- **[[perception\|Perception]] (2 stock) the Super Kamehameha** when you can't dash clear; it eats the beam and can flip into a Smash counter. Otherwise **dash sideways** through the obvious beam tell for free.
- **Sparking is your damage engine.** Teen Gohan gets extra stat boosts in Sparking; pop it, land a short rush, and finish on a **boosted super** (≈×1.2–1.5). Budget ~2 rotations.
- **Quick-kill what-if ("Unleashed Rage" → Peace Reclaimed):** beat Cell *before the timer*. Skip defense, go full aggression with Sparking + super on every opening.

### Stage 6 — Dabura, and the "defeat quickly" window

**[[dabura\|Dabura]]** is a moderate fight; the only catch is the **A Dependable Son** what-if, which needs him dead **before the time limit**. He has no regen, so just open in Sparking and dump a boosted super on him — he folds inside the window.

### Stage 9 — Defend Earth from the Frieza Force! (the no-damage gate)

This is the **fussiest trigger in the campaign**. To branch into **The Strongest Warrior**, you must beat the soldiers and **[[golden-frieza\|Golden Frieza]]**, then *finish having taken minimal / low damage* — the game grades your **remaining health**, not time. Tactics:

- **Play it safe, not fast.** Lead with **[[perception]]** on Frieza's Golden Death Beam/supers (2 stock each) and keep distance; one greedy combo that gets blocked into a counter can blow the no-damage grade.
- **Heal between phases if available.** Any banked regen or item helps you re-cross the threshold.
- **End the Frieza phase with a clean Ultimate** from a knockdown so he can't chip you back during the finisher.

### The Strongest Warrior route — Fused Zamasu finale

The 9-stage Sparking Episode walks SSJ2/Beast-tier Gohan through Goku Black and Zamasu and ends on **[[fused-zamasu\|Fused Zamasu]]** → **[[fused-zamasu-half-corrupted\|Half-Corrupted Fused Zamasu]]**. Zamasu's wall is his **"Immortal Body" — he regenerates an estimated ~20% of max HP** *(community estimate; enemy HP/regen are not datamined)* — so attrition fails; you must **out-burst the regen**:

- **Open aggressive in [[sparking-mode\|Sparking]]:** two **20+-hit rush rotations into an Ultimate** can erase a bar faster than he heals it. Don't let the fight stall into a poke war where regen catches up.
- **Respect Holy Wrath / Blades of Judgment / Lightning of Absolution** — these energy supers **stagger your bar**; **dash or [[perception]]** them, don't tank them.
- **Use [[vanish-z-counter\|Vanishes]] and [[sonic-sway\|Sonic Sway]] to make openings** when he counterattacks — a frame-perfect Super Perception vs his rush triggers Sonic Sway (dodge + counter + drains his ki).
- Full Fused-Zamasu tactics also appear on the [[episode-future-trunks\|Future Trunks page]].

## Sparking episodes & 100% completion

Gohan has **one Sparking Episode** plus several minor alternate stages; 100% requires all of them.

| Sparking Episode | Trigger | Length |
|---|---|---|
| **The Strongest Warrior** | In *Defend Earth from the Frieza Force!*, beat (Golden) Frieza and finish at **minimal damage**. (Also unlocks **Pan (GT)**.) | 9 stages |

**Minor alternate stages / scenes:**

| Stage | Trigger | Unlocks |
|---|---|---|
| *Unleashed Rage* | Defeat [[perfect-cell\|Cell]] in a short span of time | "Peace Reclaimed" |
| *Dabura* | **Defeat [[dabura\|Dabura]] before the time limit** | "A Dependable Son" |
| *Lust for Power* | Defeat [[super-buu\|Super Buu]] quickly | Exclusive scene |
| *Astonishing Team* | Defeat [[dyspo\|Dyspo]] quickly | Exclusive scene |

**Missables:** the **minimal-damage** gate on Stage 9 is the easiest 100% step to fail — if you take chip damage you fall onto the canon branch and must **replay** the stage clean to unlock The Strongest Warrior. The quick-defeat scenes (Unleashed Rage, Dabura, Lust for Power, Astonishing Team) each need a separate replay if you didn't hit the timer first time.

**Notable unlocks:** **Videl** ("Chaos at the World Tournament"), **Spopovich** ("Lust for Power"), **Pan (GT)** ("Defend Earth from the Frieza Force"), **Bojack** ("Combined Power").
