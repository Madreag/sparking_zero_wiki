**Goku Black's campaign** has the **shortest main line**, confined to **chapter 6** (the Future Trunks arc) and **available from the start**. Each new mission pays **15,000 Zeni** on first clear. The twist: **you play the villain**, so every battle is a **2-on-1 handicap** with the AI controlling the heroes — this page tracks **13 documented battles** (main line + the *Proof of Justice* Sparking Episode + the Goku-saga Zamasu mirrors logged for cross-reference).

## Walkthrough & boss strategies

Because you're outnumbered in nearly every fight (SSGSS Goku **and** Vegeta, or Vegito **and** Trunks), this campaign is harder than its length suggests. The two community walls are the **time-limited 2-on-1s** and **beating Vegito before the forced Fused-Zamasu transformation**.

### Stage 2 — Act Three Begins (the ~2:30 fast-win gate)

To branch into **Proof of Justice**, you must **defeat both [[goku-super-super-saiyan-god-super-saiyan\|SSGSS Goku]] and [[vegeta-super-super-saiyan-god-super-saiyan\|SSGSS Vegeta]] within ~2:30**. Two strong duelists on a clock means efficiency over flash:

- **Bank to [[sparking-mode\|Sparking]] immediately,** or carry **[[instant-sparking\|Instant Sparking]] (4 skill stock)** to skip the gauge and pop it on demand. In Sparking your actions are ki-free and you ignore ki-blast stun — vital when two enemies are shooting you.
- **Focus ONE target to death first.** Don't split damage. Goku Black's rush → △ → **Black Kamehameha / Work of the Gods** removes a target fast; kill one, then the fight is a winnable 1-on-1.
- **Two 20+-hit Sparking rush rotations into an Ultimate** per target is the proven quick-kill pace (the YouTube speed clears use exactly this).
- **[[revenge-counter\|Revenge Counter]] (R3, 2 stock)** when the off-target jumps in and combos you — break it and re-isolate.

### The fuse / no-fuse split (The Ultimate Fusion → Their Choice)

The campaign's signature branch decides whether you become **[[fused-zamasu\|Fused Zamasu]]**. In **The Ultimate Fusion** / **Their Choice** you fight **[[vegito\|Vegito (SSGSS)]] + [[future-trunks-super-saiyan\|Future Trunks]]**:

- **Win WITHOUT transforming** → "Responsibility as a God" / "Proof of Justice" (the heroic-irony endings).
- **Fuse into Fused Zamasu** → "The Universe Gains a New Legend."

Beating **Vegito before you're forced to fuse** is its own wall (GameRant ranks it among the hardest): Vegito has a huge health pool and a strong defensive game. **Vanish-punish** rather than rush blindly — *"create openings by [[vanish-z-counter\|vanishing]] and punish with as much damage as you can output before he responds."* Use Black Black's supers as the **punish**, not the opener. If you intend the no-fusion ending, you must close both health bars **before** the fusion prompt forces you over — so prioritise raw burst (raw Ultimate beats long-combo-into-Ultimate due to scaling).

### Playing AS Fused Zamasu (fusion branches + cross-saga mirrors)

On the fusion side you wield **[[fused-zamasu\|Fused Zamasu]]** with **"Immortal Body" (recovers an estimated ~20% of max HP — community figure, not datamined)** — now it's working *for* you, so you can afford to trade. Against Vegito/Trunks, **lead with [[sparking-mode\|Sparking]] rush rotations into Lightning of Absolution**; your regen covers any chip you take. The Goku-saga "Into a Third Future" mirror (logged here for cross-reference) is the *reverse* fight — there you face Fused Zamasu and his regen works against you; see the [[episode-future-trunks\|Future Trunks page]] for out-bursting it.

## Sparking episodes & 100% completion

Goku Black has **one Sparking Episode** (*Proof of Justice*) and a **fuse/no-fuse ending split**; full completion needs **both endings of the split** plus the route.

| Branch | Trigger | Result |
|---|---|---|
| **Proof of Justice** (Sparking Episode) | In *Act Three Begins*, defeat all enemies in ~2:30. | Opens the 4-stage *Proof of Justice* route. |
| **No-fusion ending** | In *Their Choice* / *Responsibility as a God*, win **without** becoming Half-Corrupted Fused Zamasu. | "Responsibility as a God" / "Proof of Justice" scene. |
| **Fusion ending** | In *Their Choice* / *The Ultimate Fusion*, **fuse** into Fused Zamasu. | "The Universe Gains a New Legend." |

**Missables:** the fuse vs no-fuse outcomes are **mutually exclusive in a single run**, and the no-fusion side demands you **out-damage Vegito before the forced-fusion prompt** — easy to miss. Reaching 100% means **replaying** *Their Choice* / *The Ultimate Fusion* to capture the other ending. The ~2:30 *Act Three Begins* gate likewise needs a clean replay if you miss the timer.

> The protagonist himself, **[[goku-black\|Goku Black]]**, is also obtainable outside this campaign — via a **Super Shenron** wish or at **Player Level 20**.
