Episode Battle is the story mode and the **best source of large first-clear Zeni** in the game. It's told across **8 character campaigns** spanning **7 chapters** (Saiyan → Namek → Android/Cell → Majin Buu → Beerus/Frieza Resurrection → Future Trunks → Universe Survival). This guide covers an efficient clear order, the what-if branches, and the standout **300,000-Zeni farm loop**.

## The 8 campaigns and clear order

| # | Campaign | Chapters | Unlock |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Goku** | Ch. 1-7 (longest — all 7) | Available from start |
| 2 | **Vegeta** | Ch. 2,3,4 | Available from start |
| 3 | **Gohan** | Ch. 3,4,5,6,7 | Available from start |
| 4 | **Piccolo** | Ch. 1,2,3,4 | Available from start |
| 5 | **Frieza** | Ch. 2,3,5,7 | Available from start |
| 6 | **Goku Black** | Ch. 6 | Available from start |
| 7 | **Future Trunks** | Ch. 6,7 | **Unlocked by completing Goku's saga** |
| 8 | **Jiren** | Ch. 7 | **Unlocked by completing Goku's saga** |

**Efficient clear order:**

1. **Goku first, always.** It's the longest campaign (all 7 chapters) *and* it **unlocks both Future Trunks and Jiren**. Clearing it first opens the two locked campaigns and teaches every arc.
2. **Vegeta → Gohan → Piccolo → Frieza** next. These overlap arcs you've already seen from Goku's route, so they go quickly. Frieza's route adds the four "revive a villain" branches.
3. **Goku Black** is a single chapter (Ch. 6) — fast.
4. **Future Trunks and Jiren** last (now unlocked). Future Trunks has the most branching what-ifs; Jiren's is short (Ch. 7).

No DLC has added a new Episode-Battle campaign through June 2026 (DAIMA content is roster-only). The confirmed 8 are Goku, Vegeta, Gohan, Piccolo, Jiren, Future Trunks, Goku Black, Frieza — **not Krillin**.

## What-if route unlock order

The **13 major what-if ("Sparking Episode") routes** branch off specific battles. They unlock two ways: **(a) dialogue choices** that alter the story, or **(b) harder secondary win conditions** ("defeat X within the time limit" / "win taking minimal damage" / "win without transforming"). The alternate win conditions are deliberately **harder than the default clear**.

| Campaign | What-If Route | Exact trigger |
|---|---|---|
| Goku | **Side by Side** | *The Earth's Mightiest Duo*: after first Raditz fight, **decline Piccolo's offer / "Go it alone."** |
| Goku | **Pushing the Limit** | *Rematch and Result*: **defeat Raditz before the time limit.** |
| Goku | **The Changing Future** | *Hyperbolic Time Chamber*: **choose "Go and help."** |
| Vegeta | **Parental Bond** | *Wind-Up Doll*: **defeat Android 18 quickly**, then **defeat Super Trunks** next stage. |
| Vegeta | **Number One Spot** | *Vegeta's Wicked Heart*: **shake off Babidi's brainwashing.** |
| Gohan | **The Strongest Warrior** | *Defend Earth from the Frieza Force!*: beat (Golden) Frieza **taking minimal damage.** |
| Piccolo | **A Mentor's Guidance** | *Faint Unease*: **step into Goku's fight vs Android 19** (note: a conflicting source says "defeat Frieza in 2 minutes"). |
| Future Trunks | **Shining Hope** | *Twisted World*: **defeat Zamasu quickly** (~1:40). |
| Future Trunks | **Inherited Pride** | *Trunks's Choice*: **stay in the present**, then defeat Vegeta, Gohan & Piccolo quickly. |
| Future Trunks | **Embarking Toward Tomorrow** | On Inherited Pride, in *Father or Teamwork?*: **go with Gohan.** |
| Frieza | **Frieza Force Joins the Fray** | *Frieza Revived*: **defeat Goku & Vegeta quickly** (then branches by who you revive). |
| Goku Black | **Proof of Justice** | *Act Three Begins*: **defeat all enemies before the timer** (~2:30). |
| Jiren | **True Strength** | *Saiyan Rampage*: **choose "Wait and See"** instead of fighting enraged Kale. |

**Tip:** if a timed/low-damage secondary objective is too hard, the **"I want to complete an Episode Battle"** Shenron wish (available only **after you fail one**) grants a **Dragon Orb** that eases a secondary objective. See [[zeni-economy-guide]] for the wish system.

## Episode Battle rewards

- **15,000 Zeni** per **new** Episode Battle mission on first clear `[community]`. This is the baseline — clearing all 8 campaigns once is a large lump of Zeni plus character/costume/illustration unlocks.
- **Duplicate or already-owned rewards auto-convert to Zeni** — which is what powers the farm loop below.

## The 300,000-Zeni "Super Saiyan Goku Awakens!" farm loop

The single best Zeni farm in PvE:

- **Replay "Super Saiyan Goku Awakens!"** (the Namek-arc mission where Goku first goes Super Saiyan).
- Its first-clear reward is the **Goku (Z-Mid) Outfit 3**. Once you already own that outfit, **every subsequent replay auto-converts the duplicate reward to 300,000 Zeni** `[community — Game8]`.
- That's **20x the 15,000 baseline** per run, from a single short, well-understood fight.

**Zeni/hour estimate:** the fight + cutscene + reward + restart loop runs roughly **3-5 minutes** per clear when you know it. At **~4 minutes/run** that's ~15 runs/hour:

> ~15 runs/hour × 300,000 Zeni = **~4,500,000 Zeni/hour** (with this single mission).

Even a conservative **6-minute** loop (10 runs/hour) yields **~3,000,000 Zeni/hour** — enough to buy a large chunk of the **~3,000,000-Zeni** full character shop in roughly an hour. This is the loop to grind before buying out the shop or the **52 BGM tracks (90,000 each, ~4.68M total)**. Full spend side in [[zeni-economy-guide]].

## Efficient completion checklist

1. **Clear Goku's saga first** to unlock Future Trunks + Jiren and learn every arc.
2. **Sweep the overlapping campaigns** (Vegeta, Gohan, Piccolo, Frieza, Goku Black) for the easy 15,000-per-mission first clears.
3. **Mop up what-if routes** using the trigger table; fail-and-wish for a **Dragon Orb** if a secondary objective is too hard.
4. **Once "Super Saiyan Goku Awakens!" is cleared and Outfit 3 owned, farm it** for **300,000 Zeni/run** (~3-4.5M/hour) to fund the shop.

For where to spend the Zeni (characters, BGM, ability items, wishes), see [[zeni-economy-guide]].
