The 2024 launch meta was defined by cheese — auto-dodge stalls, infinite heals, ascend-loops. Four patch waves (Dec 2024, the 2025 majors, Dec 2025, and the **May 26, 2026** overhaul) systematically dismantled most of it. This board gives the **current status** of every notorious tech: how it worked, the nerf patch with numbers, and whether it's still viable as of the May 26, 2026 patch.

> **Confidence: community** for the fine-grained numbers. Bandai patch notes are mostly **qualitative** ("reduced", "increased"); exact figures (AIS 15s→10s, instant-Sparking 3→4) are corroborated by community patch-breakdowns and cross-checked against official wording. The structural facts (what got nerfed, when) are official.

## Status board

| Tech | How it worked | Nerf patch + numbers | Current viability (May 26, 2026) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Afterimage Strike (AIS)** | Blast 1 skill: **3 skill stocks** to auto-dodge **every melee attack** for a duration — at launch a near-unbeatable stall. | **Dec 11 2024:** duration **15s → 10s**, ends if opponent lands a **Perception**. **Jun 23 2025:** also ends if opponent lands a **Super Counter**. **Dec 15 2025 (community):** deleted on a **Perception** counter, 10s confirmed. **May 26 2026:** confirmed "ends if user is hit by a Super Counter"; small buff — can now evade unblockable charge Blasts. | **Nerfed but alive.** Costs **3 stocks / 10s**, and **ends early on a Super Counter**. No longer oppressive, but a real tool against players who can't Super Counter. |
| **Wild Sense** | Blast 1 skill: **2 skill stocks** for a permanent **1-hit** auto-dodge + counter (consumed on trigger); spammable mid-combo escapes. | **Jan 20 2025:** attack damage **reduced**; can **no longer be used to continue a combo with a Rush-In**. **Dec 15 2025:** heavily restricted — community verdict "Wild Sense is GONE"; can no longer auto-dodge mid-combo continuation. | **Gutted (Dec 2025).** Now situational. The reliable mid-combo escape is dead; it's a niche 1-hit counter only. |
| **Auto-dodge (UI Goku / Whis)** | Passive/skill auto-evasion of melee — UI Goku and Whis could ignore rush pressure entirely while punishing. | **Apr 21 2025:** UI Goku Smash Ki Blast cooldown up; Whis Symphonic Destruction High-Speed-Evadable. **May 26 2026:** **Rush Chain can be executed even while attacks are auto-dodged** (explicitly names UI Goku & Whis); UI Goku Smash Ki Blast cooldown **increased** again; Whis Symphonic Destruction evadable. | **Counterable now.** Both remain **S-tier in Singles** but are no longer un-pressurable — **Rush Chain beats the auto-dodge**. |
| **Instant Sparking combos** | Skill to instantly enter [[sparking-mode\|Sparking! Mode]] (skipping the gauge), enabling surprise ult/boosted combos off neutral. | **Jan 20 2025:** cost **3 → 4 skill stocks**. **Dec 15 2025:** instant-Sparking cost reaffirmed at **4**. **May 26 2026:** **Sparking! activation now reduces defense for a window** (same on Full-Ki-Recovery skills). | **Nerfed.** Still usable at **4 stocks**, but popping Sparking is now **riskier** — you're exposed on activation. See [[instant-sparking]]. |
| **Yajirobe Senzu heal-stall** | **Senzu Bean** skill = **full HP restore**; Yajirobe (a cheap tank) looped it to stall indefinitely. | **First patch (Oct 2024):** Senzu cost **5 → 6 skill stocks**, **starting stock 2 → 0**, HP cut ~½ bar. **Apr 21 2025 / May 26 2026:** Yajirobe (+ Roshi, Mr. Satan) **Ki-recovery speed reduced**. | **Dead as a stall meta.** Senzu costs **6 stocks from a 0-stock start**, so the infinite loop is gone. In **Singles**, standardized HP removes his tank value entirely — niche DP body only. |
| **Android 19 / Dr. Gero ascend-stall** | Dragon Dash + Quick Ascend/Descend cost **no ki** for these androids → infinite vertical stall (got two finalists **DQ'd** in Nov 2024). | **Dec 11 2024:** Dragon Dash, Quick Ascend, Quick Descend **now consume Ki**; throw absorption adjusted. | **Killed (Dec 2024).** The free-stall is gone — ascending/descending costs ki like everyone else. (As a small re-buff, their Super Perception vs a beam now triggers Sparking! Mode.) |
| **Giant meta (wall-splat / AoE lock)** | Giant-class characters (Anilaza, Orange Piccolo Giant, Dr. Wheelo, Great Ape Vegeta, Cell Max) used hyper-armor + AoE + grab-immunity to wall-lock opponents. | **Dec 2024:** giant Z-Burst tackle guardable; Smash charge time up, power down. **Apr 21 2025:** rush/smash damage **reduced**, whiff recovery **up**, **takes more melee damage**, slower back-dash. **May 26 2026:** same nerfs locked cross-platform; **standard charge Blasts now damage + knock back giants** (previously whiffed). | **Nerfed.** Giants do **less damage, recover slower, take more melee**, and can be **knocked back by normal charge Blasts**. Still threatening vs unprepared opponents, but the giant-wall meta is broken. |
| **UI Goku / Whis zoning (Smash Ki Blast)** | Spammable homing/spread Smash Ki Blasts to zone from range with little risk. | **Apr 21 2025:** UI Goku Smash Ki Blast cooldown up; UI variants deflectable; unblockable variants cost more Ki. **May 26 2026:** Rush Ki Blast damage **reduced** + harsher consecutive scaling; UI Goku cooldown up again. | **Nerfed.** Ki-blast spam does less and scales worse; UI Goku zoning is taxed by cooldowns. Still strong characters, just not free zoners. |
| **Unblockable-ult / unblockable-Smash setups** | Unblockable blasts (e.g. Cell Max Max Bomb made unblockable) and unblockable Smash Ki Blasts as guaranteed mix-up enders. | **Jun 23 2025:** unblockable Smash Ki Blasts made **Vanish-evadable**, tracking reduced. **Sep 22 2025:** general unblockable Rush Blasts now **evadable with auto-evasion skills (Afterimage etc.)**. **Jan 26 2026:** Cell Max **Max Bomb → unblockable** (a buff). **May 26 2026:** auto-evasion skills can now evade **unblockable charge Blasts**. | **Mostly answerable.** Unblockables are now **Vanish-evadable and auto-evasion-evadable** — the guaranteed-damage version is largely gone. Cell Max Max Bomb is unblockable but Cell Max itself is giant-nerfed. |
| **Panic-Sparking (defensive burst)** | Popping Sparking! / Full Ki Recovery on defense to reset a bad situation safely. | **Jun 23 2025:** instant-Sparking + full-Ki-recovery skills **reduce defense for a period after activation**. **May 26 2026:** activating **Sparking! lowers defense for a window** (and same on Full Ki Recovery). | **Nerfed.** Sparking is no longer a safe panic button — you're **exposed on activation**. Time it on offense, not defense. See [[sparking-mode]]. |
| **Vanish-war spam (Z-Counter fishing)** | Mashing vanishes to win exchanges and farm skill stocks. | **Dec 11 2024:** Z-Counter input window **shortened**, harder consecutive, **Ki consumed on success increased**, **skill gain reduced**. **Jan 20 2025:** further consecutive nerf. | **Toned down.** Vanishing costs **≈half a bar per vanish** `[community]`, escalates in cost, and grants **fewer skill stocks** — you can't fish for free. Whoever has more ki still wins the chain. See [[vanish-z-counter]]. |
| **Super Counter mash-fishing** | Mashing ↑+Rush during combos to fish the **free** ~2-frame reversal. | **Jan 20 2025:** failed attempts incur a **longer lockout** (~12f cooldown, community). **Sep 22 2025 / May 26 2026:** **ki no longer recovers during a Super Counter attempt**. | **Toned down.** Still **free with a ~2f window**, but mashing now whiffs entire strings and **bleeds your ki economy**. Precision over spam. See [[super-counter]]. |
| **Perception spam (reactive parry)** | Holding [[perception\|Perception]] to parry on reaction, repeatedly, at low cost. | **Jan 20 2025:** consumes Ki on activation, recovery up. **Apr 21 2025:** vs Smash/Rush-Chain cost **1 → 2 skill stocks**; can't use during guard stun. **May 26 2026:** re-locked cross-platform — Ki cost up, follow-up **1 → 2 stocks**, no guard-stun use. | **Reworked — harder to spam.** Costs **2 stocks** vs Smash/Rush-Chain and is **banned during guard stun**, so reactive parry-spam is expensive. Rewards precise reads. See [[perception]]. |

## How to read this

- **Dead/killed:** Android 19/Gero ascend-stall, Yajirobe senzu *stall*. Don't build around these.
- **Gutted:** Wild Sense. Effectively retired as a meta tool.
- **Nerfed but alive:** Afterimage Strike (3 stocks/10s, ends on Super Counter), Instant Sparking (4 stocks), Giants, UI/Whis auto-dodge (loses to Rush Chain), unblockable setups (now evadable). Usable, no longer dominant.
- **Toned-down resource sinks:** vanish-war spam, Super Counter mashing, Perception spam — all now cost real resources.

The throughline of the **May 26, 2026** patch: nearly every defensive cheese now **costs more, exposes you, or has a counter**. For the broader patch context see `research/03`; for the live tier consequences see [[current-meta-tier-list]].
