This is the wiki's own analysis: we cross-referenced every tiered fighter's **datamined chassis
traits** (see [[health-and-damage]] and the [Roster DNA page](/dna)) against the synthesized
June-2026 tier placements. The patterns are strong enough to be predictive.

## 1. The current state in one paragraph

The **May 26, 2026 update** — the biggest balance overhaul since launch — deliberately shifted the
game from *spam* to *precision*: [[smash-and-rush|Rush Chain]] now connects through auto-dodge
(the direct counter to UI Goku and Whis), Rush Ki Blast spam was nerfed, auto-reflect was gated to
**DP 7+**, heal-on-transform was removed in Singles, and [[perception]] / deliberate
[[z-burst-dash]] play got rewarded. The post-patch S-tier (Vegito SSGSS 152.8k, Gogeta GT SS4
151.5k, both Brolys ~150k, Jiren FP, Whis, UI Goku, Gogeta Super SSGSS, Kale Berserk, Gohan Beast,
Toppo, Super Vegito, Fusion Android 13) survived **not because of gimmicks but because of their
engines** — see the fingerprint below.

## 2. The S-tier fingerprint (datamined traits × tier)

Share of fighters in each tier bucket carrying the trait — S-tier (13) and A-tier (16) vs C/D (26)
and unranked (140):

| Trait | S | A | C/D | Unranked | Read |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Elite ki recovery (≥2,000/s) | **46%** | 12% | 4% | 6% | **the strongest meta predictor — 7.7× baseline** |
| Heavy HP (45,000) | **38%** | 31% | 4% | 4% | 9.5× baseline; bulk wins the long game |
| High starting ki (3+ bars) | **54%** | 31% | 31% | 17% | round-start pressure advantage |
| Explosive Wave super in kit | **31%** | 12% | 8% | 6% | the quiet tech: point-blank AoE answers post-patch pressure |
| Rush-class attacks in kit | 77% | 75% | 58% | 64% | top tiers convert defense into cinematic damage |
| Transformation ladder | 23% | **69%** | 35% | 61% | A-tier = ladder climbers; S-tier = already-final forms |
| Max **4** skill stocks (vs 6) | **0%** | 12% | **58%** | 33% | a hard ceiling: the stock cap is a tier floor |
| Low HP (30–35k) | 0% | 0% | 31% | 11% | hard exclusion from top tiers |
| Unblockable S1 skill | 8% | 6% | **31%** | 17% | gimmicks live on weak bodies — compensation, not power |
| Android engine (no charge) | 8% | 0% | 8% | 4% | only Fusion 13's stats overcome it |

**The thesis:** after May 26, the meta selects for *resource engines*, not tricks. Every pillar of
the S-tier either regenerates ki at angel rates (Whis/Beerus 2,000–2,250/s), starts with a
material lead (3-bar starts), tanks at 45,000 HP, or all three. Meanwhile the two traits
over-represented at the bottom — the 4-stock cap and unblockable crutches — mark fighters the
game itself built as gimmicks.

## 3. Attack-category mix at the top

Among S/A-tier kits the category split is Rush-dominant (77%/75%) with **Explosive Wave** as the
stand-out over-representation (31% of S vs 6% of unranked) — in a meta where [[super-counter]]
mash got lockouts and approach dashes are distance-priced, a zero-startup AoE that punishes
point-blank pressure is disproportionately valuable. Beam/Fire shares mirror the roster average:
projectile class does **not** predict tier; the engine does. (Full category census on the
[blasts index](/blasts) — 9 classes, Rush 44% of all attacks.)

## 4. Why each S-tier survived the patch (one line each)

- **Vegito SSGSS / Super Vegito / Gogeta lines** — final-form fusions: top damage on a 6-stock,
  high-start-ki body with no ladder tax in Singles.
- **Broly (Z) LSSJ / Broly (Super) SSFP** — 45,000 HP + armor pressure; the permanent-ATK
  transform change (replacing heals) plays into them.
- **Whis / Beerus-adjacent picks** — 2,250/2,000 recovery engines. Whis **keeps** his passive
  auto-dodge, but it is no longer un-pressurable: the May-26 Rush Chain change makes
  [[smash-and-rush|Rush Chain]] connect through automatic evasion `[community]`, so melee now
  has a key into him — he holds S on the resource engine, not the dodge.
- **UI Goku** — nerfed (Smash Ki Blast cooldown, Rush Chain pierces the dodge) yet the
  auto-dodge + 2,000/s engine still clears the bar.
- **Jiren FP / Toppo** — 2,000/s recovery, standardized-HP Singles favors their attrition kits.
- **Kale Berserk** — 45k HP berserker damage on a cheap ladder (3 stocks from base).
- **Gohan Beast** — the dual-meta outlier: elite recovery + free Beast Sense read; #1 DP value
  (33.9k) *and* top-10 Singles.
- **Fusion Android 13** — the one android that beats the no-charge tax: 45k HP, 2,000/s recovery,
  Giant-class armor without Giant DP pricing.

## 5. Watchlist — chassis says yes, tier list hasn't caught up

Computed: every non-S/A fighter carrying **3+ traits from the S-cluster** (elite recovery, 45k HP,
3-bar start, auto-dodge skill, transform/fusion access). Our top sleeper calls, with reasoning:

| Fighter | DP | Cluster traits | Why it could be meta |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Super Janemba** | 7 | 2,250/s recovery (Whis-tier!), 3-bar start, unblockable | The only non-angel with angel recovery + a teleport (Dimension Shift); post-patch precision meta suits him. Strongest sleeper case. |
| **Perfect Cell** | 7 | 2,000/s recovery, auto-dodge, 3-bar start | Already B and climbing — the patch's zoning-precision reward fits his kit; expect A. |
| **Golden Frieza** | 8 | auto-dodge, 3-bar start, ladder | Engine profile matches A-tier transformers; DP8 pricing is the drag. |
| **Gotenks (→SS3)** | 6→8 | 45,000 HP line, unblockable at SS3, ladder | One of only 16 heavies; ladder costs are low; Singles HP-equalization helps less here — DP sleeper. |
| **Syn Shenron** | 7 | 3-bar start, unblockable, ladder to Omega | Omega endpoint is already rated; the base is underrated transit. |
| **Videl** | 2 | 2,000/s recovery, auto-dodge | Singles D is fair — but at DP2 with an elite engine she's a **DP-queue value pick**, not a throw slot. |

The inverse holds too: anything carrying the **4-stock cap** (68 fighters, including most movie
villains) has a datamined ceiling — don't expect patch notes alone to lift them without a stock
change.

## 6. Method

Tier placements: synthesized June-2026 sources ([/meta](/meta) board). Traits: datamined per
fighter (this wiki's extraction, build 22517964). Buckets: S=13, A=16, C/D=26, unranked=140
playable slots. Percentages are within-bucket trait prevalence; "×baseline" compares S vs
unranked. Patch context cross-checked against the games.gg May-26 rundown and sparkingzerometa's
current-patch list on June 10, 2026.
