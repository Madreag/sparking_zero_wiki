Sparking! ZERO now runs **two different balance models**, so there is no single tier list — there are two. The **May 26, 2026 Free Update** (the largest balance patch since launch) split the game: **Single Battle** uses **standardized HP and no DP damage scaling**, while **DP Battle** **widened** the strength gap between high- and low-DP characters. A character can be **S in Singles and C in DP** (Whis, Jiren, SS4 Gogeta) or the reverse (Master Roshi, Yajirobe, Recoome). The two tables below reflect that split exactly as `research/05` presents it.

## As-of tagging and stale sources

- **As of:** the **May 26, 2026** Free Update (latest build referenced ~June 5-8, 2026). Confidence: **community** — these are model scores and community consensus, not official rankings.
- **Most current quantitative source:** `sparkingzerometa.com` (scored, "reflects current patch"). Scores in parentheses are **model points** (higher = better), used as **relative ranking signals**, not raw in-game damage.
- **EXCLUDED as stale:** the **Game8 tier list is frozen at "as of November 2024"** and is **not used** in this synthesis. It predates every 2025-2026 balance patch (the AIS nerfs, the June 2025 HP-standardization, the giant nerfs, and the May 26 2026 overhaul) and would actively mislead.
- **Known lag:** `sparkingzerometa.com` notes it is mid-update, so the freshly-nerfed DP picks (Roshi/Yajirobe/Recoome Ki recovery; Recoome/Spopovich/Nappa HP) and nerfed Singles picks (UI Goku, Gogeta Super SSGSS) are expected to **drift down** on the next refresh.

## Why the two lists diverge so hard

| Mechanic (May 26, 2026) | Effect on tiers |
|---|---|
| **Singles: DP damage scaling removed, HP standardized** | Cheap tanks lose their edge → Yajirobe/Roshi crater in Singles; raw-kit fusions dominate. |
| **Singles: heal-on-transform removed → permanent attack buff** | Transforming Saiyans (Broly, Kale, Gohan Beast) gain a lasting offense buff instead of a panic-heal. |
| **DP: DP gap widened in all modes** | High-DP gods hit even harder vs cheap bodies, but cost the same DP — efficiency picks still win the budget. |
| **Auto-reflect locked to DP7+** | Cheap sub-DP7 turtle shells lost their auto-reflect, slightly taxing the "3x5-DP value spam" archetype. |
| **Giant nerfs (less damage, more whiff recovery, take more melee)** | Giant-class picks (Anilaza, Orange Piccolo Giant, Dr. Wheelo, Cell Max) drop from the late-2025 giant meta. |

For the team-building consequences and exact HP-per-DP figures, see [[dp-team-value-math]]. For the ranked ladder this list feeds into, see [[ranked-climbing-guide]].
