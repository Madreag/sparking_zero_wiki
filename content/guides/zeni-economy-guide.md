Zeni is the single offline currency, and it has **no documented hard wallet cap**. This guide covers what you earn per mode, what the big spend sinks cost, and how to play the **Shenron wish** system for maximum payout. Bottom line: the spend side is **enormous** (full collection is well over 7M Zeni), so farm the high-value loops.

## Earn rates per mode

| Activity | Zeni | Confidence |
|---|---|---|
| **Bonus Battle — Low-Class Warrior** first clear | **20,000** | community |
| **Bonus Battle — High-Class Warrior** first clear | **25,000** | community |
| **Bonus Battle — Elite Saiyan Warrior** first clear | **30,000** | community |
| **Bonus Battle — Legendary Super Saiyan** first clear | **35,000** | community |
| **Episode Battle** — new mission first clear | **~15,000** | community |
| **"Super Saiyan Goku Awakens!" replay** (Outfit 3 → duplicate conversion) | **300,000 / run** | community |
| Shared/Custom "special battle" clear | ~10,000 | community (label ambiguous) |
| Offline/CPU quick win (farm) | ~1,000 | community |
| Online ranked / player match win | unconfirmed | gap |

**Bonus Battles** are the developer-authored custom battles (preset characters, win conditions) — there are **~37 total** across the four difficulty classes (10 Low + 14 High + 10 Elite + 3 Legendary), and they **double as character-unlock content**. Clearing them once nets the tiered Zeni above **plus** roster unlocks (Bardock, Bergamo, Frieza Force Soldier, etc.). Some Bonus Battles chain-unlock others.

**The big earner** is the Episode Battle farm loop — **300,000 Zeni/run** from replaying "Super Saiyan Goku Awakens!" once you own its outfit reward. See [[episode-battle-completion]] for the full loop and Zeni/hour math (~3-4.5M/hour).

## The spend side

### Characters (~3,000,000 Zeni for the full shop)

- **47 base characters** are shop-purchasable (the other ~110 of 182 are free from start or via PVE; the rest unlock through Episode/Bonus battles, tournaments, or wishes).
- **Buying one base character unlocks ALL its variants/transformations/fusions** — you never pay per form.
- **Per-character price band: 30,000 - 120,000 Zeni.**

| Example character | Shop price |
|---|---|
| Videl | 30,000 |
| Goku (Teen) | 45,000 |
| Kale | 60,000 |
| Goku (GT) / Goku Black | 75,000 |
| Frost / Dyspo | 90,000 |
| Majin Vegeta | 105,000 |
| UI Goku -Sign- / Gogeta (GT) SS4 | 120,000 |

- **Total to buy the entire shop roster ≈ 3,000,000 Zeni** `[community — PowerPyx]`. In practice you spend far less because many unlock free via PVE.
- **Goku (Mini)** is the one base-roster character **not** buyable in-game (pre-order bonus DLC).

### BGM (52 tracks x 90,000 = 4,680,000 Zeni)

The single **largest Zeni sink** in the game:

- **52 BGM tracks**, **90,000 Zeni each** → **4,680,000 Zeni** for the full set `[community — Game8]`.
- Many are gated behind **Player Level (0-16)** in addition to the Zeni cost.

### Ability items (15k / 45k / 150k tiers)

Stat-boost equippables for combat, in three price tiers `[community]`:

| Tier | Price | Example items |
|---|---|---|
| **Tier 1** | **15,000** | Melee Charge 1, Ki Blast Charge 1, Combo Master, Super Transformation, Sparking! Plus, Latent Power Unleashed 1 |
| **Tier 2** | **45,000** | King Kai Training, Rush Attack Boost 2, Energy Saver 2, Blast Attack Boost 2 (+30 more) |
| **Tier 3** | **150,000** | God of Destruction Training, Rush Attack Boost 3, The Secret to Counters, The Secret to Dragon Dashes, God Ki (+20 more) |

### Total collection cost (rough)

| Sink | Cost |
|---|---|
| Full character shop | ~3,000,000 |
| All 52 BGM | 4,680,000 |
| Ability items (full set, many tier 1-3) | several hundred thousand+ |
| **Grand total (characters + BGM alone)** | **~7,680,000+ Zeni** |

This is why the **300,000/run farm loop matters** — the full collection is roughly **2-3 hours** of focused farming on that single mission.

## Shenron wishes

Collecting **Dragon Balls in battle** summons one of three dragons, each needing its own set of 7. They're an **alternative unlock path** and a Zeni source.

| Dragon | Summons | Difficulty | Zeni wish payout |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Shenron** | 1 wish | Easiest | **300,000** |
| **Porunga** | **3 wishes** per summon | Mid-rarity | **120,000** (per wish, so up to 360,000) |
| **Super Shenron** | most options | Rarest (tied to Episode/saga completion) | **1,000,000** |

> The **300k / 120k / 1,000,000** split is cross-checked across GameRant, Gameland.gg, PCGamesN, PowerPyx, and Destructoid — all agree.

### Other wish options (not Zeni)

- **Raise Player Level** (random level-ups; useless once at **L20 cap**)
- **More titles** (Player-Card cosmetics; up to **11** options across dragons)
- **New outfits** (Shenron/Super Shenron only; choose 1 of **4**: Desert Bandit Yamcha, Jackie Chun, Goku Super outfit 4, Vegeta Super outfit 4)
- **Ability items** (Porunga/Super Shenron only; choose 1 of **6**)
- **More characters** (Super Shenron only: unlock **Goku Black** or **Fused Zamasu**)
- **Complete an Episode Battle** (Shenron only, **after failing one** — grants a Dragon Orb; see [[episode-battle-completion]])

## Optimal wish strategy

1. **If you only want Zeni, prioritize Super Shenron** for the **1,000,000** single payout — it's the rarest but pays **3.3x a Shenron wish** and **8.3x a single Porunga wish**.
2. **Porunga is the value pick for non-Zeni goals** — its **3 wishes per summon** mean you can grab an ability item *and* a title *and* 120,000 Zeni from one dragon, vs Shenron's single wish.
3. **Use Shenron's Zeni wish (300,000) freely** as the easiest dragon — it's a fast, repeatable top-up, on par with one farm-loop run.
4. **Spend ability-item and outfit wishes on Porunga/Super Shenron** (Shenron can't grant ability items), and save the **"more characters"** wish for Super Shenron only if you haven't already bought Goku Black / Fused Zamasu at L20.
5. **Don't waste a "raise Player Level" wish at L20** — it does nothing once capped.

## Earn-then-spend priority

1. **Clear Episode + Bonus battles once** for the 15k-35k first-clear lumps and the free character unlocks (see [[episode-battle-completion]]).
2. **Farm "Super Saiyan Goku Awakens!"** for 300k/run to bankroll everything.
3. **Buy characters you actually play** (gated by [[current-meta-tier-list]] / [[dp-team-value-math]]) before sinking 4.68M into BGM.
4. **Use wishes** as a free supplement — Super Shenron for the 1M Zeni hit, Porunga for breadth.
