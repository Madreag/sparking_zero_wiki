**Ver.2009.008.003.006.005** (March 4, 2025) was a **minor balance / maintenance patch** focused on the limited-time boss mode and one transform fix.

## Changes

- **Legendary Warrior Face-Off:** fixed a [[broly-super|Broly (Super)]] status-reduction bug and "adjusted battle balance."
- **[[orange-piccolo|Orange Piccolo]] transform line** ([[piccolo|Piccolo (Super Hero)]] / Power Awakening → Orange Piccolo → [[orange-piccolo-giant-form|Giant Form]]): the **first transform of the match now restores HP and Ki** — a buff/fix.
- Otherwise performance and UX only.

## Meta impact

Negligible at the roster level — no character-vs-character damage changes shipped. The [[orange-piccolo|Orange Piccolo]] first-transform heal restored intended behavior for that line. Note this transform-heal mechanic would itself be removed game-wide in the September 22, 2025 transformation overhaul.
