**Ver.2014.013.003.009.008** (September 22, 2025) is the **transformation-overhaul** patch, shipped alongside DAIMA Pack 2 early access. It reshaped what a transforming character is worth.

## Transformation overhaul (system-defining)

- **Characters that healed on transformation NO LONGER heal.**
- **Transforming via Skill Stock now grants a PERMANENT attack-power buff** (removed on revert) — this redefines the value of every transform character ([[gohan-beast|Gohan Beast]], [[gogeta-super-super-saiyan-god-super-saiyan|SSGSS]], UI lines).

## Battle system

- **[[super-counter|Super Counter]]:** Ki no longer auto-recovers during the move — a direct cut to its defensive value (now reflected on the [[super-counter|Super Counter]] page).
- **Short Dash:** a second short dash allowed immediately after one (once). **Ascent/Descent:** faster at close range. **Quick Ascent/Descent:** Ki cost increased.
- **Ultimate Blasts (general):** combo damage-reduction eased (Ultimates do a bit more in combos).
- **Ki Blast Deflect → Smash:** now fast. **[[perception|Perception]]:** follow-up chargeable by holding.
- **[[revenge-counter|Revenge Counter]]:** fixed the Perception-vs-Revenge-Counter window being too short.
- **Paralyzing Smash Ki Blast:** Ki cost up; Revenge Counter usable when hit from front; two combo hits knock away instead of paralyzing.
- **Unblockable Rush Blasts:** now evadable with auto-evasion skills ([[afterimage-strike|Afterimage]] etc.).

## Character

- **[[gogeta-super-super-saiyan-god-super-saiyan|Gogeta (Super) SSGSS]]:** stationary Smash Ki Blast attack power **increased** — a partial buff after the April nerf.
- **[[whis|Whis]]:** "Overture to Destruction" less likely to hit directly above/below.

## Ranked & content

Minimum **50 points per match** regardless of rank-point gap; top 1–100 player & character ranks earn text titles. **First Demon World** stage added; option to display the battle-area edge.

## Meta impact

Removing transform-healing and replacing it with the permanent-ATK-buff model rebalanced the entire transforming archetype, and [[super-counter|Super Counter]] losing Ki regen reduced its defensive value. Community meta writeups for this patch are thinner than April's, but the transform change is foundational — it is the same model the May 26, 2026 update later brought to all platforms in Single Battle.
