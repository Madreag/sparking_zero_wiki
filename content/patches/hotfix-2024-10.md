The launch-week activity in **October 2024** was a run of **stability, matchmaking and visual hotfixes** — there is no official balance changelog for this window, and the "v1.0.1.0" label is a community convention rather than a Bandai version string.

## What is confirmed

- Online matchmaking / stability and visual fixes only.
- No published gameplay or balance changes.
- Bandai's only explicit "first update" wording from this era is the Switch visual-improvement note, which actually belongs to the 2025 Switch launch.

## The contested Yajirobe nerf

Research/05 (community meta history) credits an **October 2024 first balance patch** with the change that killed the Senzu-stall meta:

- **[[yajirobe|Yajirobe]] Senzu cost: 5 → 6 skill stock**
- **Starting skill stock: 2 → 0**
- **HP reduced** ("best to worst overnight")

Research/03 — the primary patch enumeration — lists **no balance** in the October hotfix window and does not assign a date to the Yajirobe change. The two sources are in tension, so the numbers above are tagged community and their exact patch placement should be considered unconfirmed.

## Meta impact

If the early-October attribution is correct, this was the first real correction to the launch meta, removing [[yajirobe|Yajirobe]] from the top of the [[senzu-bean|Senzu]]-stall tier. Either way, by the time the **December 11, 2024** balance patch landed, the Senzu infinite-heal stall was no longer a defining strategy.
