The **December 15, 2025 patch** has two faces. The **official note** is a motion-control tuning and giant-bugfix pass; the **community** remembers it as the patch that finally gutted [[wild-sense|Wild Sense]].

## What the official note says

- **Options:** Motion-Control play style gains 3-level sliders for "ease of performing/stopping Rush Attacks" and "ease of Smash Attacks."
- **Burst Smash:** fixed failing to hit at close range for **giant characters**.
- **Great Ape Baby (GT)** Rush Attack (Motion Control): fixed follow-up hits not landing. **[[great-ape-vegeta|Great Ape Vegeta]]** Super Galick Gun: fixed distance-dependent whiff.
- **Skill descriptions:** fixed [[afterimage-strike|Afterimage Strike]] and [[wild-sense|Wild Sense]] **description-vs-effect mismatches** — a text fix, explicitly not a balance change.
- No roster damage changes; a QoL / stability pass.

## What the community reports

Community patch-breakdowns (research/05) credit this update with substantive nerfs:

- **[[wild-sense|Wild Sense]] heavily restricted** — "Wild Sense is GONE"; can no longer auto-dodge mid-combo continuation.
- **Instant-Sparking cost: 3 → 4 skill points.**
- **[[afterimage-strike|Afterimage Strike]] 15s → 10s**, deleted on a [[perception|Perception]] counter.
- **Force / [[false-courage|False Courage]] 1 → 2**; netcode stability improved.

> These numbers come from a community video breakdown, not the captured official text. Some — the [[afterimage-strike|AIS]] 15s→10s cut and the [[false-courage|False Courage]] 1→2 increase — are officially documented for the **December 2024** patch, so their Dec 2025 restatement should be read as community memory rather than a fresh change. The instant-Sparking **3→4** and the Wild Sense gutting are the genuinely new community-attributed items here.

## Meta impact

Whether by this patch's text fix or a quieter effect tweak, the community consensus is that December 2025 is when **most launch-era cheese was finally tamed**: [[wild-sense|Wild Sense]] became situational, and popping instant-Sparking got costlier. The May 26, 2026 update then locked in the broader anti-Sparking and giant changes cross-platform.
