**Ver.2011.010.003.007.006** (April 21, 2025) was the first **major balance** patch — a sweeping **mobility + defense-tool nerf** that slowed neutral and trimmed the dominant cast.

## Battle system — movement & neutral

- **Short Dash:** backward speed reduced; Dragon-Dash-from-Short-Dash recovery reduced.
- **High-Speed Dragon Dash:** easier Rush-Attack connect; reduced Smash charge time; guarantees reaching the opponent's front despite trajectory manipulation.
- **Z-Burst Dash:** activation Ki cost reduced, now scales Ki by distance travelled.
- **General melee:** more Ki recovery on hit; less targeting deviation vs Step. **First Ki gauge:** faster auto-recovery. **Rush-In:** fixed guard being possible between Rush-In and Rush Attacks.

## Battle system — defense

- **[[perception|Perception]]:** cannot perform during guard stun; follow-up window increased; **activation Ki cost increased**; **skill-count requirement raised 1 → 2** for Smash Attacks and Rush Chains — the headline Perception nerf.
- **[[perception|Super Perception]]:** stronger + longer deflect buff, slight Ki restore on deflect.
- **Auto-reflect:** only **DP 7+** characters can activate it in Sparking! Mode.
- **Auto-evasion skills:** cancelled if [[perception|Perception]] is used during activation — continued [[afterimage-strike|AIS]] / [[wild-sense|Wild Sense]] crackdown.

## Blast / Ki

- **Rush Ki Blast:** damage down + stronger consecutive-hit scaling. **Smash Ki Blast (spread):** damage down; [[goku-super-ultra-instinct|UI Goku]] variants now deflectable; unblockable variants cost more Ki + recovery.
- **Blast / Ultimate in combos:** damage reduced; consecutive-Blast damage reduced.
- **[[future-trunks-super-saiyan|Future Trunks (Super)]] Final Flash:** Ki cost + damage up. **[[super-trunks|Super Trunks]] Finish Buster:** damage up. **[[whis|Whis]] Symphonic Destruction:** now High-Speed-Evadable.

## Character

- **Giant characters (general):** Rush & Smash damage reduced; whiff recovery up; **melee damage taken increased**; backward Short Dash slower — the biggest giant nerf yet.
- **[[master-roshi|Master Roshi]] / [[mr-satan|Mr. Satan]] / [[yajirobe|Yajirobe]]:** Ki recovery speed reduced.
- **Androids (general):** auto Ki recovery stops during Z-Counter; silhouette hidden with Z-Search off.
- **[[goku-super-ultra-instinct-sign|UI Goku -Sign-]] / [[goku-super-ultra-instinct|UI Goku]]:** Smash Ki Blast cooldown increased.
- **[[dr-wheelo|Dr. Wheelo]]:** max Skill Count reduced. **[[nappa|Nappa]] / [[recoome|Recoome]] / [[spopovich|Spopovich]]:** health reduced.
- **[[gogeta-super-super-saiyan-god-super-saiyan|Gogeta (Super) SSGSS]]:** Smash Ki Blast damage & homing reduced.

## Meta impact

This is the **mobility + defense nerf** patch. [[perception|Perception]] 1→2 made reactive parrying much costlier, the DP7+ auto-reflect lock killed cheap-character turtling, and the dominant cast — [[yajirobe|Yajirobe]] Ki regen, [[goku-super-ultra-instinct|UI Goku]] zoning, [[gogeta-super-super-saiyan-god-super-saiyan|SSGSS Gogeta]], and giants — all took direct hits. Many of these exact changes were later re-locked cross-platform in the May 26, 2026 overhaul.
