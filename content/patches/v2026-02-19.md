The **February 19, 2026 free update** (versions NS2 `2A013.012.003.009.011` / NS `2013.012.003.009.011`) was the **Switch / Switch 2 companion patch**, aligned with the February 20 Switch DLC drop.

## What it adds

- **Extreme Warrior Time Attack** — a limited-time mode where you fight super-buffed enemies.
- **Battle Together:** all stages selectable (only on a Switch 2 with the update).
- Custom Battle scenario / cut-in / text additions; usability and stability fixes.

## Meta impact

None at the cross-platform competitive level — there are **no PS5/Xbox/PC balance changes** in this entry. It is a Switch-side content companion update. The cross-platform balance picture stayed put until the May 26, 2026 overhaul.
