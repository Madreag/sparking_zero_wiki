**"DAIMA Character Pack 2"** (full release September 25, 2025; early access September 21) shipped alongside the September 22 transformation-overhaul patch.

## What it adds

**6 fighters:**

- [[goku-mini-super-saiyan-4|Goku (Mini) SSJ4]], [[goku-daima-super-saiyan-4|Goku (DAIMA) SSJ4]]
- [[vegeta-daima-super-saiyan-3|Vegeta (DAIMA) SSJ3]]
- [[majin-duu|Majin Duu]], [[third-eye-gomah|Third Eye Gomah]], **[[giant-gomah|Giant Gomah]]**

## Meta impact

The two SSJ4 mini-Gokus added high-end DAIMA Saiyan options, while **[[giant-gomah|Giant Gomah]]** joined the giant roster and fed directly into the late-2025 giant meta that the December 2025 and May 2026 patches would later nerf.
