**"DAIMA Character Pack 1"** (April 24, 2025) was the second paid character pack, drawing entirely from *DRAGON BALL DAIMA*.

## What it adds

**8 fighters:**

- [[goku-mini-super-saiyan|Goku (Mini) SSJ]]
- [[vegeta-mini|Vegeta (Mini)]], Vegeta (Mini) SSJ, Vegeta (Mini) SSJ2, **Vegeta (Mini) SSJ3**
- [[glorio|Glorio]], [[panzy|Panzy]], [[majin-kuu|Majin Kuu]]

Plus a Goku (Mini) technique-changing costume.

## Meta impact

Mostly a roster-expansion drop, but **Mini Vegeta SSJ3** became a notable DP-value pick — cheap for its output on the points-per-DP model that DP Battle rewards. The rest of the pack is mid-roster or niche.
