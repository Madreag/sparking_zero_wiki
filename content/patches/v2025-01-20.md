**Ver.2008.007.003.005.004** (January 20, 2025) was the **system patch that prepped DLC1**, and it opened the long campaign to tame Perception and the auto-dodge skills.

## Battle system

- **[[perception|Perception]] / [[perception|Super Perception]]:** now **consume a set amount of Ki** on activation, with longer post-move recovery — the first Perception nerf.
- **[[super-counter|Super Counter]]:** the lockout after a **failed** attempt is **increased**, widening the whiff-punish window (mashing ↑+square now whiffs entire strings).
- **Speed Impact:** re-input timing pushed later.
- **Chase Change:** reduced damage from Giant characters when chase-changing into them.
- **Rush Ki Blast:** can no longer High-Speed Evade while being hit. **Smash Ki Blast:** easier to get hit if fired while moving. **Charge Attacks:** no recoil while charging when hit by Rush Attack / Ki Blast in Sparking! Mode.

## Skills

- **[[wild-sense|Wild Sense]]: attack damage reduced.**
- **All auto-avoidance skills (Wild Sense etc.):** opponents **can no longer use them while continuing a combo with a Rush-In** — a major anti-[[afterimage-strike|AIS]] / Wild Sense change.
- **[[instant-sparking|Instant Sparking]]: skill-count cost 3 → 4** — the increase that makes a panic-Sparking pop cost nearly a full stock bank.
- **[[kaioken|Kaioken]]:** skill-count cost reduced (Goku Z-Early only). **[[ta-dah|Ta-dah!]]:** cost reduced. **[[all-i-need-is-five-seconds|All I Need Is Five Seconds!]]:** Ki now fully recovered.

## Modes & content

Legendary Warrior Face-Off (limited-time boss mode) added; Quick Match reworked to instant-match; ranked disconnect penalty wait-time added; training "Guarding After Attacks" option.

## Meta impact

This patch began the multi-patch effort to dismantle auto-dodge degeneracy and reactive [[perception|Perception]] spam, and paved the way for the DLC1 "Hero of Justice Pack" three days later. The Perception Ki cost and the Super Counter whiff-lockout (see [[super-counter|Super Counter]]) are the changes that show up most in high-rank play.
