On **November 14, 2025**, Sparking! ZERO **released on Nintendo Switch and Switch 2** in Standard, Deluxe and Ultimate editions.

## What shipped

- A **"Software Update Data Patch"** (announced November 7, live November 14) upgrades the Switch cartridge for **enhanced visuals on Switch 2**.
- **No balance changes.**

## Meta impact

None at the gameplay level — this is a platform expansion. The cross-platform balance picture would not change until the December 15, 2025 patch (a QoL/bugfix pass) and, decisively, the May 26, 2026 cross-platform overhaul. Switch / Switch 2 later received their own companion content updates (February 19, 2026) and stage parity (First Demon World via the May 26, 2026 update).
