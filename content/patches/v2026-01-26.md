The **January 2026 patch** (January 26) paired a content wave with light Ki-economy tuning.

## Modes

"Ultimate Battle" added to the main menu (Custom Battle moved inside it); **Mission 100** solo mode; **"Ultra" CPU difficulty** above Super; Custom Battle max CPU level **→ 21**.

## Battle system

- **[[perception|Super Perception]]:** more Ki recovery on deflect.
- **Rush Ki Wave:** now reduces the opponent's Ki when guarded.
- **Jump Rush Blast:** Ki consumption reduced. **Dash Rush Ki Blast:** Ki consumption reduced for characters firing **7+ shots**.
- **Vanishing Attack:** easier for tall characters to land follow-ups on grounded foes.
- **Action Lock during Ki Depletion:** certain counter actions disabled while out of Ki; mash-UI now shows correctly.

## Skills & Blasts

- **[[solar-flare|Solar Flare]]:** no longer ends when approached via Dragon Dash / Z-Burst Dash.
- **[[cell-max|Cell Max]]: Max Bomb → UNBLOCKABLE** — the notable buff of the patch.
- Forward projectile/beam hitbox rear-adjusted; point-blank charge/rush Blast consistency fixed; some Z-Search-off Blasts toggled.

## Meta impact

Mostly Ki-economy and consistency tuning, with the [[cell-max|Cell Max]] Max Bomb unblockable change as the one standout combat shift. Community impact was light — this was a QoL + new-mode wave more than a balance reset.
