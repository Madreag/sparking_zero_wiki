The **"Hero of Justice Pack"** (January 23–24, 2025) was the first paid character DLC, drawing from *Dragon Ball Super: SUPER HERO* and early DAIMA.

## What it adds

**11 playable fighters:**

- [[gohan-super-hero|Gohan (Super Hero)]], Gohan (Super Hero) SSJ, [[ultimate-gohan|Ultimate Gohan (Super Hero)]], **[[gohan-beast|Gohan Beast]]**
- [[piccolo|Piccolo (Super Hero)]], Piccolo (Super Hero) Power Awakening, **[[orange-piccolo|Orange Piccolo]]**, [[orange-piccolo-giant-form|Orange Piccolo Giant Form]]
- **[[gamma-1|Gamma 1]]**, **[[gamma-2|Gamma 2]]**, **[[cell-max|Cell Max]]**

Plus a costume and 3 bonus battles.

## Meta impact

[[gohan-beast|Gohan Beast]] arrived strong, but the community verdict was that it was "the right amount of broken to fight top tiers, not enough to shift the meta." [[goku-super-ultra-instinct|UI Goku]], [[gogeta-gt-super-saiyan-4|SSJ4 Gogeta]], [[yajirobe|Yajirobe]] and [[afterimage-strike|Afterimage Strike]] users stayed at the top of the ladder. [[cell-max|Cell Max]] joined as a giant, foreshadowing the late-2025 giant meta.
