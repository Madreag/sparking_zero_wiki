The **May 26, 2026 free update** is the **largest single balance patch in the game's history** and the current build as of the June 10, 2026 cutoff. It brings the cross-platform balance picture in line with the late-2025 PS5/Xbox/PC changes and pushes the Singles/DP split further apart. (Switch strings shown: NS2 `2A013.012.003.009.011`; applies cross-platform.)

## System-defining

- **Single Battle:** transform characters **no longer heal on transform**; instead gain a **permanent ATK buff via Skill Stock** (removed on revert) — extends the September 2025 model to all platforms.
- **[[dp-system|DP Battle]]:** DP differences between characters made **more pronounced** in all modes; **total DP selectable 10 / 15 / 20** (settings retained).
- **First Demon World** stage added (Switch parity).

## Battle system

- **Skill Stock:** auto-recovery speed slightly reduced.
- **Z-Burst Dash:** initial Ki cost reduced, then Ki by distance. **Rapid Ascend/Descend:** Ki cost increased. **High-Speed Dragon Dash:** smoother rush connect, reduced Smash charge time.
- **[[perception|Perception]]:** cannot use during guard stun; wider follow-up window; **Ki cost up**; **skill-stock requirement 1 → 2** for Smash Attacks & Rush Chain; follow-ups chargeable — mirrors and locks in the April 2025 Perception nerf cross-platform.
- **[[super-counter|Super Counter]]:** auto Ki recovery **disabled** during it.
- **[[perception|Super Perception]]:** stronger + longer buffs; slight Ki on deflect.
- **Rush Ki Blast:** damage reduced + stronger consecutive scaling. **Smash Ki Blast (spread):** damage reduced; [[goku-super-ultra-instinct|UI Goku]] variants deflectable.
- **Blast Attacks:** combo & consecutive-hit damage reduced; rapid-fire Ki Blast damage increased; **standard-vs-giant charge Blasts now deal damage + knockback**. **Ultimate Blast:** combo damage-reduction eased.
- **Auto-reflect:** only **DP 7+** in Sparking! Mode.
- **Stun-type Smash Ki Blast:** Ki cost up; [[revenge-counter|Revenge Counter]] usable from front; double-hit knocks away instead of paralyzing.

## Skills

- **Buff skills ([[saiyan-spirit|Saiyan Spirit]], [[pump-up|Pump Up]]):** duration increased. **[[turles|Turles]] [[fruit-of-the-tree-of-might|Fruit of the Tree of Might]]:** bigger boost + Ki recovery.
- **Instant-Sparking skills:** **defense reduced for a period after** — anti-panic-Sparking.
- **Full-Ki-recovery skills:** defense reduced for a period after. **[[afterimage-strike|Afterimage Strike]]:** ends if hit by a **[[super-counter|Super Counter]]**.
- **[[dyspo|Dyspo]] [[super-maximum-light-speed-mode|Super Maximum Light Speed Mode]]:** reduced Short-Dash Ki cost + icon. **[[kale|Kale]] [[cry-of-rage|Cry of Rage]]:** activation time up.

## Character

- **Giant characters (general):** rush & smash melee damage reduced; whiff recovery up; **melee damage taken increased**; backward Short Dash slower — the definitive cross-platform giant nerf.
- **[[master-roshi|Master Roshi]] / [[mr-satan|Mr. Satan]] / [[yajirobe|Yajirobe]]:** Ki recovery reduced.
- **[[dr-gero|Dr. Gero]] / [[android-19|Android 19]]:** Super Perception vs beam/projectile now triggers Sparking! Mode, stat-boost removed.
- **[[goku-super-ultra-instinct-sign|UI Goku -Sign-]] / [[goku-super-ultra-instinct|UI Goku]]:** Smash Ki Blast cooldown increased (and Rush Chain can now be executed even while their attacks are auto-dodged).
- **[[dr-wheelo|Dr. Wheelo]]:** max Skill Stock reduced. **[[nappa|Nappa]] / [[recoome|Recoome]] / [[spopovich|Spopovich]]:** health reduced.
- **[[gogeta-super-super-saiyan-god-super-saiyan|Gogeta (Super) SSGSS]]:** Smash Ki Blast damage & homing reduced, stationary Smash Ki Blast power increased.
- **[[future-trunks-super-saiyan|Future Trunks (Super)]] Final Flash:** Ki cost + damage up. **[[super-trunks|Super Trunks]] Finish Buster:** damage up. **[[whis|Whis]] Symphonic Destruction:** High-Speed-Evadable.

## Meta impact

A sweeping rebalance that **locks in giant nerfs, [[perception|Perception]] 1→2, instant-Sparking defense-down, and the [[dp-system|DP]]-spread system across all platforms.** It targets the long-standing menaces — giants, [[yajirobe|Yajirobe]] Ki regen, [[goku-super-ultra-instinct|UI Goku]] zoning, [[gogeta-super-super-saiyan-god-super-saiyan|SSGSS Gogeta]], and panic-Sparking — and finishes splitting Singles (standardized HP, no DP scaling, raw-kit fusions on top) and [[dp-system|DP]] (value-per-point, cheap overperformers) into genuinely different games. This is the patch the current tier lists are built on.
