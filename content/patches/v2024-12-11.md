The **December 11, 2024 free update** was the game's **first real balance pass**, and it went straight for the two loudest launch complaints: auto-dodge degeneracy and the infinite-stall exploit.

## System & mechanic changes

- **Z-Counter (Vanish counter):** input window shortened, harder to chain consecutively, more Ki consumed on success, less Skill Count gained — a broad defensive-vanish nerf.
- **[[revenge-counter|Revenge Counter]]:** [[perception|Perception]] used against it now costs **1** Skill Count instead of **2**.
- **Z-Burst Dash:** activation Ki cost increased; **giant Z-Burst tackles are now guardable.**
- **Burst Smash:** no activation Ki cost; instead drains Ki by distance travelled.
- **Rush Attacks in Sparking! Mode:** stronger consecutive-hit damage scaling (combos do less).
- **Character Switch:** standby-character HP recovery reduced.
- **New action:** High-Speed Dragon Dash (hold R2 + cross / RT + A).

## Skills (with numbers)

- **[[afterimage-strike|Afterimage Strike]]: 15s → 10s**, and now ends if the opponent lands a [[perception|Perception]] on you — the first dent in AIS dominance.
- **[[false-courage|False Courage]]: skill-count cost 1 → 2.**
- **[[all-i-need-is-five-seconds|"All I Need Is Five Seconds!"]]: cost 3 → 2** with a bigger activation stat boost.

## Character changes

- **[[android-19|Android 19]] / [[dr-gero|Dr. Gero]]:** Dragon Dash, Quick Ascend and Quick Descend now consume Ki — this kills the infinite ascend/descend stall — and throw HP/Ki absorption was adjusted.
- **[[great-ape-vegeta|Great Ape Vegeta]] / [[dr-wheelo|Dr. Wheelo]]:** max health reduced.
- **Giant characters (general):** Smash Attack charge time up, power down.
- **[[cooler|Cooler]]:** transform HP restore increased. **[[cell-2nd-form|Cell 2nd Form]]:** throw now escapable.

## Meta impact

This patch **killed the [[android-19|Android 19]] / [[dr-gero|Dr. Gero]] stall** — the exploit that got two finalists DQ'd in November 2024. The [[afterimage-strike|Afterimage Strike]] 15s→10s cut plus the Perception-cancel was the first real check on AIS, but AIS and [[wild-sense|Wild Sense]] stayed top-tier, and the giant nerf was mild. Community consensus treats this as a strong opening salvo rather than a meta reset.
