**Ver.1.00** — the October 11, 2024 launch of DRAGON BALL: Sparking! ZERO — shipped the base game with a **182-character roster** and the full Budokai Tenkaichi 3-style mechanic suite that every subsequent patch has been tuning.

## What launched

- **Roster:** 182 characters in the base game.
- **Modes:** Episode Battle, Custom Battle, Battle, World Tournament, online Ranked and Player Match.
- **Core systems (the baseline):** Sparking! Mode, Vanish / Z-Counter, [[super-counter|Super Counter]], [[revenge-counter|Revenge Counter]], [[perception|Perception]] / [[perception|Super Perception]], [[afterimage-strike|Afterimage Strike]] & [[wild-sense|Wild Sense]], and the Skill Count / Stock economy.

## The numbers (community-measured baseline)

Bandai published no raw frame or gauge data at launch. The community-measured baselines that later patches moved are:

- **[[afterimage-strike|Afterimage Strike]] duration: 15 seconds** — dodges all melee but not Ki blasts. Cut to 10s in December 2024.
- **[[super-counter|Super Counter]]: ~12-frame input cooldown** between attempts; the success window itself is frame-tight.
- **Vanish / Z-Counter:** a fixed Ki chunk per use (~1 bar commonly cited; exact "25 Ki" figures are unverified).

## Meta impact

The launch meta is the "Wild West" era. [[afterimage-strike|Afterimage Strike]] spam, [[goku-super-ultra-instinct|UI Goku]] / [[whis|Whis]] auto-dodge, the [[yajirobe|Yajirobe]] Senzu infinite-heal stall, and the [[android-19|Android 19]] / [[dr-gero|Dr. Gero]] ascend-descend stall all dominated ranked. DP-based damage scaling also made high-DP single characters disproportionately strong — a problem not fully addressed until the June 23, 2025 overhaul. These four degeneracies are what the entire patch history below was written to fix.
