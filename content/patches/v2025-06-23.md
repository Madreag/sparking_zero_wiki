**Ver.2013.012.003.008.007** (June 23, 2025) is the **system-defining** patch — the single biggest competitive shift since launch — and it is where Singles and DP began to diverge into genuinely different games.

## Fundamental DP / health overhaul

- **Single Battle: REMOVED damage scaling based on DP** in all modes. This ends the "high-DP hits harder" era.
- **All characters set to the SAME health** in single battle (online and offline).
- **[[dp-system|DP Battle]]:** rebalanced so DP-cost differences are **more pronounced** (Team rules unchanged).

## Ranked system (with numbers)

- **Seasonal system with resets.** End-of-season demotion: **Z→B1, S→B3, A→B5, B→C5, C→D5, D→D5.**
- **Win-streak bonus (max 5): 2 wins ×1.2, 3 ×1.4, 4 ×1.8, 5 ×2.0.**

## Battle system

- **Skill Counts:** auto-recovery slightly reduced; no longer recovers on a successful tech; low-HP bonus only triggers below the **last** health bar; Attack-Break gain increased.
- **Unblockable Smash Ki Blasts:** now Vanish-evadable; tracking slightly reduced.
- **Instant-Sparking & full-Ki-recovery skills:** **defense reduced for a period after activation** — a recurring anti-panic theme.
- **[[afterimage-strike|Afterimage Strike]]:** now ends if the opponent lands a **[[super-counter|Super Counter]]** — another AIS counter stacked on top of the Perception-cancel.
- **Buff skills ([[saiyan-spirit|Saiyan Spirit]], [[pump-up|Pump Up]]):** duration increased. **[[turles|Turles]] [[fruit-of-the-tree-of-might|Fruit of the Tree of Might]]:** bigger boost + Ki recovery.

## Character

- **[[gohan-super-hero|Gohan (Super Hero)]] & SSJ:** health reduced. **Gohan→[[ultimate-gohan|Ultimate Gohan]]:** more Skill Count to transform, HP restored on transform. **Ultimate Gohan→[[gohan-beast|Gohan Beast]]:** Skill Stock to transform **increased** — a Gohan Beast access nerf.
- **[[dr-gero|Dr. Gero]]:** Super-Absorption vs beam / Ki blast now triggers Sparking! Mode and the stat-boost effect is removed.

## Meta impact

The **equalized-HP + no-DP-scaling** change is the largest competitive shift in the game — it reset character-vs-character damage math and made many mid-DP picks viable in Singles, while the [[dp-system|DP Battle]] change pushed value toward cheap overperformers. [[gohan-beast|Gohan Beast]] got harder to reach. This is the patch that split the game into two balance models, a split the May 26, 2026 update would later widen further.
