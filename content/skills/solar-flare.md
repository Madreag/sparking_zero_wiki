## In-game description *[datamined]*

> Unguardable attack. Renders opponent defenseless for (N) seconds.

Solar Flare blinds the opponent, briefly interrupting their inputs to open a punish or escape window. The January 26 2026 patch buffed it so that it no longer ends when the opponent rushes in with a Dragon Dash or Z-Burst Dash — previously, dashing through cancelled the blind; now it persists, making it a more reliable neutral disruption tool.
