## In-game description *[datamined]*

> HP fully recovers.

Senzu Beans is Yajirobe's signature skill: a **full health restore**. At launch it cost **5 skill stock** and Yajirobe began the match with 2 stock, enabling a notorious infinite-heal stall. The very first balance patch (late Oct 2024) raised the cost to **6 stock**, set his starting stock to **0**, raised his max skill count 5→6, and shaved about half a health bar — a change widely described as taking him 'from best to worst overnight'. The May 26 2026 patch further reduced his Ki-recovery, and because Single Battle now uses standardized HP, his tank value there is gone; he survives only as a niche DP-Battle body.
