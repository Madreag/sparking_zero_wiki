## In-game description *[datamined]*

> Ki gauge fully recovers; melee attack power, Ki Blast attack power, and Blast power greatly increase for (N) seconds. Defense decreases for a period of time.

'All I Need Is Five Seconds!' is a burst self-buff. The December 11 2024 update cut its Skill Count cost from **3 to 2** and increased the activation stat boost; the January 20 2025 patch then added **full Ki recovery** on activation, turning it into a strong tempo/comeback button for the Trunks line.
