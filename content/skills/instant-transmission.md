## In-game description *[datamined]*

> Instantly move behind opponent.

Instant Transmission is a teleport reposition skill (cost character-specific) used to escape pressure or close/create distance. Its exact skill-stock cost is not cleanly datamined. After the May 26 2026 Z-Burst Dash rework (initial Ki cost lowered but now scaling with travel distance), Instant Transmission is cited as one of the explicit counters to full-screen Z-Burst approaches alongside Dragon-Dash descend and switch-just-before-hit.
