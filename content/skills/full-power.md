## In-game description *[datamined]*

> Enter Sparking! Mode; defense greatly decreases for a period of time.

Full Power is a self power-up activation. Its strategic value shifted with the September 22 2025 transformation overhaul: in Single Battle, transforming via Skill Stock no longer heals but instead grants a **permanent attack-power buff** (lost on revert) — a model re-locked across all platforms in the May 26 2026 update. This reframes power-up skills as a lasting offense investment rather than a panic-heal.
