## In-game description *[datamined]*

> Automatically dodge a single attack of any kind, except for Ki Blasts or Perception/Super Perception/Super Counter counterattacks, for (N) seconds. Dodge effect will end after being hit by a Perception/Super Perception/Super Counter counterattack.

Afterimage Strike is the most patched skill in the game. At launch it cost **3 skill stock** and granted a **15-second** window that auto-dodged *every* melee attack (but never Ki blasts), making it the defining cheese of the "Wild West" meta. The Dec 11 2024 update cut the duration to **10 seconds** and made it **end when the opponent lands a Perception**; the Jun 23 2025 patch added a second hard counter (**ending it on a Super Counter**); and by the May 26 2026 patch it is firmly a situational tool rather than an oppressive one — though that same patch let it evade unblockable charge Blasts. A circulating "single-dodge per activation" claim is false: both the datamine and official text describe continuous melee evasion for the full 10s.
