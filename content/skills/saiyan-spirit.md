## In-game description *[datamined]*

> Melee attack power, Ki Blast attack power, Blast power, and damage inflicted from consecutive hits (such as combos) increase for (N) seconds.

Saiyan Spirit is a timed self-buff. Both the June 23 2025 and May 26 2026 patches increased buff-skill durations (Saiyan Spirit and Pump Up are explicitly named), lengthening the effective window of the boost.
