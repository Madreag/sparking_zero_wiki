## In-game description *[datamined]*

> Creates a ki explosion, blowing away opponents.

Explosive Wave is a close-range area-of-effect burst that knocks back surrounding opponents and clears incoming ki blasts, used as a get-off-me / space-reset tool. Research notes both **2-cost and 3-cost** variants exist depending on the character. No specific balance changes to it are documented in the official patch notes through June 2026.
