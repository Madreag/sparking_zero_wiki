## In-game description *[datamined]*

> Automatically dodge a single attack of any kind and counterattack, except for Ki Blasts or Perception/Super Perception counterattacks. Dodge effect will end after being hit by a Perception/Super Perception counterattack.

Wild Sense is a **2 skill stock** reactive defense that auto-dodges a single incoming melee attack and counters, consuming the buff on trigger. Unlike Afterimage Strike (a timed multi-dodge), it is a one-shot read. It was a launch-era staple; the Jan 20 2025 patch cut its counter damage and barred its use while continuing a combo via Rush-In, the Apr 21 2025 patch let Perception cancel it, and the Dec 15 2025 patch restricted it so severely that the community declared it effectively dead for combo-continuation defense.
