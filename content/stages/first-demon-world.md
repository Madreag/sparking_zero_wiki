**First Demon World** — stage entry from the game's map table.

| Map ID | Variant |
|---|---|
| `0020` | First Demon World |

First Demon World is the DRAGON BALL DAIMA arena added as a **free update alongside DAIMA Character Pack 2** (Sept 22-25 2025 on PS5/Xbox/PC; rolled out to Switch and Switch 2 with the May 26 2026 update). Like the other open battle arenas it has destructible terrain (blasts carrying the `Map Destruction` flag damage the environment). It is the only new stage delivered during the Season Pass 1 DLC window; the next batch (Kami's Palace, Stratosphere/Planet Vegeta and two more) is tied to the unreleased Super Limit-Breaking NEO expansion.
