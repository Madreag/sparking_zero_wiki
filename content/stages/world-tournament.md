The World Tournament stage is the ring-based arena from the World Martial Arts Tournament. Certain ring-out-style ultimate attacks carry a datamined `World Tournament Evacuation` flag, which forces a crowd/ring evacuation sequence — a stage-specific interaction unique to the tournament maps.
