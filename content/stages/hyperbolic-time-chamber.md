**Hyperbolic Time Chamber** — stage entry from the game's map table.

| Map ID | Variant |
|---|---|
| `0006` | Hyperbolic Time Chamber |

The Hyperbolic Time Chamber is the endless white void from the Cell/Buu training arcs — a flat, featureless arena with no destructible structures. Research notes it is excluded from the 'Power of Earth' damage item that applies on normal Earth maps, consistent with it being a neutral, hazard-free space.
