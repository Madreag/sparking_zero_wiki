**Special Beam Cannon** is a **Beam**-class Super (Blast 2) costing **30,000 ki energy**.
- **Chargeable** — hold the input to charge for more damage *[datamined]*
- Also exists as a separate **ultimate** for other fighters: [[special-beam-cannon-ultimate\|Special Beam Cannon (ultimate)]]
